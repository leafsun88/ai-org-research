# Essence Format Tests 2026-04-21

## Test 1 — Lovable 用轻 cadence 保住速度，三个月 roadmap 只做方向锚

Lovable 的 weekly planning 很轻，核心是把一周真正的 bottleneck 摆到同一块 FigJam board 上：哪些问题最影响产品体验，哪些东西本周必须 ship，哪些路线只需要先给方向。demo 把所有人拉回真实交付，Linear 甚至被拿来做 talent application tracking，说明这家公司还没有把“工具”按职能切死。三个月 roadmap 在这里更像方向锚，一个月后变样也正常；AI 应用公司的底层变量变化太快，过重流程会制造假确定性，过轻节奏会滑向 chaos。Lovable 的组织选择是固定 cadence、保留弹性，用很少的人减少 context loss。

“we have like a fig Jam board where we have all the main problems and then we have kind of rank them” —— Anton Osika, Lenny's Podcast, 2025-03-09

“we have a demo where we say like okay this are this the things we Shi this week so they get everyone on the same page” —— Anton Osika, Lenny's Podcast, 2025-03-09

“we do Talent application tracking in linear” —— Anton Osika, Lenny's Podcast, 2025-03-09

“it stretches up three months and then but with with in one month it's probably going to look a bit different” —— Anton Osika, Lenny's Podcast, 2025-03-09

## Test 2 — Midjourney 10 人团队同时做 12 条线，时间表天然漂移

Midjourney 的组织矛盾被 David Holz 讲得很直：团队只有约 10 人，手上同时有下一代模型、当前模型 fine-tune、web creation flow、organization flow、specialized model、moderation、Discord 规模化等 12 条线。小团队让 Midjourney 有极高资本效率，也让创始人持续贴着社区；代价是每条线的 time scale 都很难预测。David 对时间的描述很有价值：明天能做什么很清楚，长期会去哪里也清楚，中间所有细节会互相叠加成 uncertainty。这个 operating condition 解释了 Midjourney 的两个表面矛盾：产品推进很快，发布日期却很少被说死。

“we're mostly you know we're only around 10 people so um it's a struggle sometimes” —— David Holz, Discord Office Hours, 2022-09-07

“we're working on like 12 things at the same time and so doing like 12 things at once means that it's harder to predict the time scale for each specific thing” —— David Holz, Discord Office Hours, 2022-09-07

“if we just did one thing we would know exactly how soon but it's just really hard right now” —— David Holz, Discord Office Hours, 2022-09-07

“it's easy to know what's gonna happen tomorrow and it's easy to know it's gonna happen in the far future and then everything in between are like all the little details that you don't know” —— David Holz, Discord Office Hours, 2022-09-07

“we kind of have to sort of diversify and uh and have more time uncertainty on each thing” —— David Holz, Discord Office Hours, 2022-09-07

## Test 3 — Lovable 的招聘是强筛选器，work simulation 比面试更接近真实组织

Lovable 的 hiring 设计很硬：先用 job description 把节奏、强度和风险说透，再用 work simulation 看候选人在真实环境里怎么做事。Anton 要找的人需要 raw cognitive capability，也要能快速 iterate、把业务当成整体来想，单一 craft 很强还不够。这个标准和 Lovable 的产品形态是同一套逻辑：AI 把执行杠杆放大后，团队更需要能判断问题、自己找路径、愿意承担不确定性的人。work simulation 至少一天，常常一整周，本质上是在筛 agency 和真实协作密度，比普通面试更接近未来 AI-native product team 的日常。

“people join the work simulation for at least a day” —— Anton Osika, Lenny's Podcast, 2025-03-09

“often a full week” —— Anton Osika, Lenny's Podcast, 2025-03-09

“long hours High Pace” —— Anton Osika, Lenny's Podcast, 2025-03-09

“those seeking comfortable work need not apply” —— Anton Osika, Lenny's Podcast, 2025-03-09

“Raw cognitive capability is the strongest” —— Anton Osika, Lenny's Podcast, 2025-03-09

“thinking about the business as a whole” —— Anton Osika, Lenny's Podcast, 2025-03-09

## Test 4 — Midjourney 的 community 在承担 onboarding，用户学习成本本身就是产品问题

Midjourney 的 office hours 里有一个很有价值的用户反馈：一个刚用 48 小时的新用户说，技术进步速度已经快过 documentation，自己靠反复试 prompt 去摸系统边界，free credits 很快被实验消耗掉。David 的回应把 Midjourney 的 community 角色讲清楚了：Prompt Craft、gallery、复制 prompt、预渲染 library 都在承担学习基础设施的功能。对 Midjourney 这种创作工具来说，learning cost 直接影响留存和付费；用户越能理解 prompt 与结果之间的关系，越容易从好奇心进入长期创作。community 在补齐模型不可解释、文档追不上、生成结果高度随机这几个结构性缺口。

“the tech is advancing faster than the documentation” —— Midjourney user, Discord Office Hours, 2022-09-07

“the feedback loop there isn't super tight” —— Midjourney user, Discord Office Hours, 2022-09-07

“you burn up those those free slots real fast” —— Midjourney user, Discord Office Hours, 2022-09-07

“one of the goals of having the community is that people can learn together” —— David Holz, Discord Office Hours, 2022-09-07

“people together actually are really good at figuring out how to do it” —— David Holz, Discord Office Hours, 2022-09-07

“making it a lot like easier and smoother and fun and more fun and cheaper just to learn” —— David Holz, Discord Office Hours, 2022-09-07
