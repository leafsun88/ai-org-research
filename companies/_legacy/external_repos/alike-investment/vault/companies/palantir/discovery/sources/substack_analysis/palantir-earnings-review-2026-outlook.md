# Palantir Earnings Review and 2026 Outlook

## Company Overview

Palantir Technologies Inc. is an enterprise software company focused on data analytics and artificial intelligence. The Denver-based firm specializes in platforms like Gotham, Foundry, and AIP that help organizations process complex datasets. Founded in 2003, it serves both government and commercial sectors globally.

## Financial Performance

In fiscal year 2025, Palantir generated approximately $4.48 billion in revenue, representing roughly 56% year-over-year growth versus 2024. This growth rate significantly outpaces major competitors in the enterprise analytics space.

## Competitive Landscape

The company faces competition from several established firms including Snowflake Inc., Splunk Inc., and Datadog, Inc., all operating within the enterprise analytics and AI software sector.

## Key Insight

Palantir has been executing and growing faster than any other company in the enterprise AI software space, positioning it as a standout performer during this period.

## 2026 Guidance

The company guided to $7.182-$7.198 billion in total revenue for fiscal 2026, representing approximately 61% year-over-year growth, beating consensus expectations of $6.22 billion.
