# The $20 Trillion Palantir Thesis

Linares presents an investment thesis that Palantir is on a path to become a $20T company and eventually $100T is also plausible. He categorizes the company as the first "Singularity Scaler" — a business whose earning potential evolves in tandem with AI advancement.

## Key Arguments

**Current Position:** Palantir mirrors Nvidia's situation around 2017 — early in its scaling phase with a digital twin monopoly and a total addressable market encompassing the entire Western economy.

**Growth Drivers:** Two factors propel free cash flow per share growth:
1. The pace of productization
2. Network effects from that productization

**Operational Momentum:** As of Q4 2025, Palantir processes approximately 1 billion API requests per week for applications built by customers on top of AIP. This represents evolution from individual digital twin provider to a network of interconnected digital twins.

**Network Expansion:** As adoption spreads across functions and industries, organizations increasingly leverage pre-configured compute solutions, reducing time-to-value while strengthening competitive advantages. This creates a pathway for Palantir to expand from powering individual companies to managing entire supply chains, industries, and nations.

**Conclusion:** Linares contends that escalating pre-configured compute capabilities make replication increasingly difficult, supporting his long-term valuation projections.
