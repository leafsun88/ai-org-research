# Palantir Stock 2026 Forecast: Is Its High Valuation Sustainable?

Palantir delivered exceptional Q3 2025 results with revenue growth accelerating to 62.8% year-over-year, marking the company's highest growth rate since going public. The analysis examines whether this performance justifies the stock's elevated 67.5 forward price-to-sales valuation.

## Key Financial Highlights

**Revenue Performance:**
- Q3 revenue reached $1.18 billion, up 18% quarter-over-quarter
- Revenue growth accelerated 14.8 percentage points to 62.8% YoY
- Full-year guidance raised to 53.5% growth

**Commercial Segment Strength:**
US Commercial revenue surged to 121% YoY growth, reaching $397 million and accelerating 28 percentage points from prior quarter. The segment showed:
- Total contract value (TCV) jumped 342% YoY to $1.31 billion
- Deals exceeding $5 million increased fivefold YoY

**Profitability Metrics:**
The company achieved a remarkable Rule of 40 score of 114% (revenue growth plus operating margin), up from 94% the previous quarter. Adjusted operating margin exceeded 50% for the first time, reaching 51%.

## Product Innovation

Palantir unveiled three major platform upgrades:

1. **AI Forward-Deployed Engineers (FDEs):** Autonomous agents that streamline data integration and Ontology creation
2. **AI Hivemind:** Orchestrates multiple agents for complex problem-solving with classified data awareness
3. **Edge Ontology:** Enables lightweight deployment on mobile devices and edge hardware

## Valuation Concerns

At 67.5x forward sales, Palantir trades substantially higher than comparable software leaders like CrowdStrike (24.7x) and Cloudflare (23.4x). The forward price-to-earnings ratio sits at 176.5, slightly below Snowflake's 180.4.

## Key Metrics

Net retention rate expanded six basis points sequentially to 134%, with 629 deals exceeding $1 million signed over the past twelve months. However, billings and remaining performance obligations (RPO) growth decelerated in Q3.

## Investment Thesis

The article emphasizes Palantir's distinctive competitive advantages, particularly its Ontology knowledge graph providing superior context compared to standard language models. Unlike traditional business intelligence platforms requiring complete datasets, Palantir operates effectively with fragmented information, offering what the company describes as the reasoning that goes into decision-making.

Citi recently upgraded Palantir to Buy with a $235 price target, citing expectations for a commercial and government supercycle driven by accelerating enterprise AI adoption and defense budget expansion.
