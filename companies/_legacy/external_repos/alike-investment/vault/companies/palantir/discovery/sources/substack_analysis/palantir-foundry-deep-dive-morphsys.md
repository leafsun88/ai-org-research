# What Does Palantir Really Do? A Deep Dive into Its Mission and Foundry Platform

Palantir Technologies is an enterprise software company founded in 2003 that specializes in data integration and operational decision-making. Palantir's commercial offering centers on Foundry, described as a platform that unifies data into a shared business model called an Ontology.

## Key Components

**Foundry Platform:** The core product transforms fragmented organizational data into actionable insights. Rather than replacing existing infrastructure, Foundry serves as an operational layer connecting disparate systems like ERP, CRM, and logistics tools without forcing complete system replacement.

**The Ontology:** This shared business model captures real entities and relationships that define how an organization operates including orders, suppliers, assets, and work orders. It enables both semantic understanding and kinetic elements like actions and security protocols.

**AIP (Artificial Intelligence Platform):** The AI layer integrates LLMs and agents into operations with built-in governance, evaluation tools, and audit trails. It moves beyond "chat over documents" toward "operational grounding."

**Apollo:** Deployment infrastructure supporting cloud, on-premises, and regulated environments.

## Target Industries

The platform suits organizations with interdependent operational constraints — airlines, manufacturers, healthcare systems, and energy producers — where coordinated decision-making across siloed data creates competitive advantage.

## When Foundry Works Best

Effective implementation requires organizations ready to align around shared data models and change management, particularly those needing governed AI capabilities beyond traditional analytics.
