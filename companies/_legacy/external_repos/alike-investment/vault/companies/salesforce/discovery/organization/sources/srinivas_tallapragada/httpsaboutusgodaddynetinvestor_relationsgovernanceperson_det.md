Srini Tallapragada

 Srini Tallapragada, President and chief engineering officer, Salesforce, Inc.

Srinivas (Srini) has been a member of GoDaddy’s board of directors since 2023. Srini has been the president and chief engineering officer at Salesforce, Inc. since December 2019. He also served as the president, executive vice president and senior vice president at Salesforce from May 2012 to December 2019. From April 2011 to May 2012, he was the senior vice president of engineering at Oracle Corporation, where he also served as the vice president and senior director of applications development from 2001 to 2009 and the director of engineering and customer relationship manager from 2000 to 2001. In addition to these roles, he served as the senior vice president at SAP Labs Inc. from February 2009 to April 2011.

Srini previously served on the board of directors of Avalara Inc. from July 2021 to October 2022 when Avalara became a private company.

He holds a PGDM from XLRI in Jamshedpur, India, and a BTech in Computer Science from the National Institute of Technology in Warangal, India.
