# Labubunomics: The Economics of Pop Mart's Labubu Craze

**Author:** Gad Allon | **Date:** 2025-08-25

## Financial Performance

- 2024 revenue: ~$2B (doubled YoY), Labubu contribution: ~$418M (23%)
- H1 2025: revenue RMB 13.88B ($1.93B), +204% YoY
- H1 2025 net income: +397% to RMB 4.57B
- The Monsters line: 700%+ annual growth in 2024

## Economic Model

Utility function: U(N) = v - p + αR(N) + μln L(N) - γN
- v = intrinsic enjoyment, p = price
- αR(N) = recognition/bandwagon effect (grows with adoption)
- μln L(N) = resale market liquidity benefit
- γN = exclusivity loss penalty (increases with ubiquity)

Key insight: Utility peaks at intermediate adoption levels (inverted-U shaped demand), NOT traditional network effects. Early adoption benefits from rising recognition + liquidity, but over-saturation erodes exclusivity premium.

## Market Saturation Analysis

Logistic diffusion model suggests revenue saturation at RMB 8-10B annually (~20-30M units globally). Beyond this threshold, exclusivity penalties outweigh recognition gains → growth deceleration after 2025.

## Distribution Strategy

- Physical retail: 21 US stores (mid-2025), expanding to 40 by year-end
- Bangkok: world's largest Pop Mart store
- Robo Shop: automated vending in malls/transit
- TikTok Shop: +1000% YoY sales growth (May 2025)
- Livestream marathon broadcasts: items sell out within minutes
- New collections released every ~30 days

## Viral Mechanics

- Lisa (BLACKPINK) endorsement April 2024 → mainstream breakout
- #Labubu: 1.6M+ posts, dominated by unboxing videos
- #1 traded brand on StockX for 8 consecutive months
- Secondary market: 3-5x retail ($20-30 retail vs $1,500-2,800 resale)
- Ultra-rare variants: six-figure auction prices

## Risk Factors

1. **Regulatory**: China restricts blind-box sales to minors; may spread internationally
2. **Counterfeiting**: widespread unauthorized replicas dilute brand
3. **Saturation**: Beanie Babies / Funko Pops precedent for sharp demand reversals
4. **Cultural translation**: Asian success ≠ sustained Western appeal

## Strategic Outlook

- ~100 new overseas locations planned for 2025
- North America expected to surpass Southeast Asia as primary international market
- Expansion into themed cafes and merchandise licensing
- Core competitive advantage: orchestrating cyclical hype, not any single IP
