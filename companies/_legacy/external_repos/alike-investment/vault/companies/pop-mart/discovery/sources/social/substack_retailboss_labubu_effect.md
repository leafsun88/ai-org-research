# The Labubu Effect: Pop Mart's Explosive Growth Analysis

**Author:** Jeanel Alvarado & RETAILBOSS | **Date:** 2025-12-14

## Key Business Metrics

- 2018 pre-Labubu revenue: $74.81M → 2025 annualized: $2.50B (33x in 6 years)
- H1 2025 revenue: RMB 13.7B (exceeded full-year 2024 in 6 months)
- H1 2025 net profit: +350% YoY
- Labubu represents 60-70% of total revenue
- Production scaled from 300K units/month (early 2024) to 10M units/month (mid-2025)
- Labubu sales growth: 1,200% from 2023 baseline

## Growth Drivers

1. Partnership with creator Kasing Lung since 2019
2. Product design resonance (sharp-teethed, fuzzy character)
3. Supply chain scaling capability (thousands → millions of units)
4. Scarcity/blind-box model driving repeat purchases
5. Market timing alignment with collectibles trend

## Competitive Moat

- Proprietary IP with exclusive character relationships
- Manufacturing expertise (demonstrated 33x production scaling)
- Distribution network (retail + DTC channels)
- Brand cult status in collector communities

## Key Risks

- **Concentration risk**: 60-70% revenue from single product line
- Market saturation and shifting consumer preferences
- Quality maintenance during massive production scaling
- International expansion complexity
- Collectibles markets are inherently trend-dependent
