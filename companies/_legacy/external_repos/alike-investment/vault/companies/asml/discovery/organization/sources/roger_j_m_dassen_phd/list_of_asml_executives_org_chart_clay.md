The leadership team at ASML is at the helm of setting the strategic direction for the company, ensuring that every decision aligns with their mission to push the boundaries of technology. They are not just decision-makers but visionaries who foster a culture of innovation and growth, making ASML a leader in the semiconductor industry.

Bringing together expertise from various fields, this team drives operational excellence and collaboration across the board. Their diverse backgrounds and unified vision enable them to navigate complex challenges and seize new opportunities, propelling ASML to new heights.

1) Christophe Fouquet

Christophe Fouquet, the President and Chief Executive Officer at ASML, is responsible for steering the company towards its strategic goals and ensuring operational excellence. His leadership is pivotal in maintaining ASML's position as a frontrunner in the semiconductor industry.

During his tenure, Fouquet has significantly contributed to the company's growth, particularly in the areas of EUV technology and applications. His efforts have been instrumental in driving innovation and expanding ASML's market reach.

With a strong background in physics from Grenoble INP - UGA and extensive experience in marketing and product management, Fouquet brings a wealth of knowledge to ASML. His previous roles at KLA-Tencor and Applied Materials have equipped him with the skills necessary to lead complex initiatives and foster a culture of continuous improvement.

2) Roger Dassen

Roger Dassen, as the Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer at ASML, oversees the company's financial strategy and operations, ensuring fiscal health and compliance. His expertise in auditing and financial management plays a crucial role in driving ASML's financial performance and strategic growth.

With a distinguished career spanning roles at Deloitte and various academic positions, Dassen brings a wealth of experience in auditing, financial oversight, and corporate governance. His tenure at Deloitte, where he served as Global Managing Director and CEO, equipped him with the skills to lead complex financial initiatives and foster a culture of excellence.

At ASML, Dassen's leadership in financial planning and risk management has been instrumental in supporting the company's innovation and market expansion. His strategic insights and robust financial acumen significantly contribute to ASML's continued success and industry leadership.

3) Jim Koonmen

Jim Koonmen, the Executive Vice President and Chief Customer Officer at ASML, is responsible for overseeing customer solutions and support, ensuring that clients receive top-tier service and innovative solutions. His contributions are pivotal in maintaining strong customer relationships and driving ASML's customer-centric strategies.

Koonmen's extensive career at ASML includes roles such as Executive Vice President of the Applications Business and CEO of the Cymer Light Source Division. His background in marketing, operations, and engineering, combined with his education from MIT, equips him with the specialized skills necessary to lead complex initiatives and foster innovation.

Managing a diverse team, Koonmen is involved in various strategic initiatives aimed at enhancing customer satisfaction and operational efficiency. His leadership in these areas significantly contributes to ASML's reputation for excellence and its continued success in the semiconductor industry.

4) Wayne Allan

Wayne Allan, the Executive Vice President and Chief Strategic Sourcing & Procurement Officer at ASML, is responsible for overseeing the company's sourcing and procurement strategies. His role involves ensuring the efficient acquisition of materials and services, which is crucial for maintaining ASML's operational excellence and innovation.

Allan brings extensive experience from his tenure at Micron Technology, where he held various senior positions, including Senior Vice President of Global Manufacturing and Vice President of World-Wide Fabs. His expertise in supply chain management and global manufacturing has been instrumental in driving ASML's strategic initiatives and supporting its growth in the semiconductor industry.

ASML Org Chart

At the top of ASML's organizational chart is Christophe Fouquet, the President and Chief Executive Officer, who oversees the company's strategic direction and operational excellence. Reporting directly to him are key executives, including Roger Dassen, the Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, and Jim Koonmen, the Executive Vice President and Chief Customer Officer, each playing pivotal roles in financial strategy and customer relations, respectively.

Wayne Allan, the Executive Vice President and Chief Strategic Sourcing & Procurement Officer, collaborates closely with the leadership team to ensure efficient procurement and sourcing strategies. Together, these executives align their efforts to foster innovation, operational efficiency, and customer satisfaction, driving ASML's mission to push the boundaries of technology in the semiconductor industry.
