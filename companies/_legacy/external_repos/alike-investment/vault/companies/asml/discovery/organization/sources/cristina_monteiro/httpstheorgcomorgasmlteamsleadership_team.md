About

The Leadership Team at ASML is responsible for setting the strategic direction and overarching vision of the company while driving operational excellence across all functions. This diverse group of executives collaborates to manage key areas such as finance, engineering, customer support, and marketing, ensuring alignment with the company's goals of innovation and market leadership in advanced microchip technology. Their collective expertise facilitates decision-making and fosters a culture of teamwork, accountability, and sustainability within the organization.
