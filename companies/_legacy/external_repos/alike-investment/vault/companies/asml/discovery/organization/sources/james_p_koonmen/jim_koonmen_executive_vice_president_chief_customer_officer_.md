Jim Koonmen has held several executive positions in various companies. Jim is currently the Executive Vice President, Head of Customer Solutions and Support at ASML since May 2006. Before that, Jim was the VP of Marketing and Operations at MEMX Inc from March 2002 to May 2006. Jim also served as the Director of Manufacturing Engineering at Onetta from August 2000 to March 2002. Jim holds a BS & MS in Aeronautics and Astronautics from Massachusetts Institute of Technology and an MS in Management from MIT Sloan School of Management.
