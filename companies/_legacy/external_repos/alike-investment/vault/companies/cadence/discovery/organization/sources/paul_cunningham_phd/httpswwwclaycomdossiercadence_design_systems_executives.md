The leadership team at Cadence Design Systems is the driving force behind the company's strategic direction. They make key decisions that align with the company's mission, ensuring every move supports their overarching goals. This team is dedicated to fostering a culture of innovation and growth, encouraging every employee to push the boundaries of what's possible.

Bringing together expertise from various fields, the leadership team excels in driving operational excellence and collaboration. Their diverse backgrounds and unified vision create a dynamic environment where ideas flourish and teamwork thrives. It's this blend of skills and perspectives that propels Cadence Design Systems forward in the competitive tech landscape.

1) Anirudh Devgan

Anirudh Devgan serves as the President and Chief Executive Officer at Cadence Design Systems, where he is responsible for steering the company's strategic vision and operational execution. His leadership ensures that Cadence remains at the forefront of innovation in the tech industry.

Since joining Cadence in 2012, Devgan has held several key positions, including Executive Vice President & GM and President, before becoming CEO in 2021. His contributions have been pivotal in driving the company's growth and fostering a culture of excellence and collaboration.

Devgan's extensive background includes a Ph.D. and M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University, and a B.Tech. in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi. His deep technical expertise and leadership experience, gained from roles at Magma and IBM, equip him with the skills necessary to lead Cadence in a competitive landscape.

2) John Wall

John Wall serves as the Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer at Cadence Design Systems, where he oversees the company's financial strategy, planning, and operations. His expertise in accounting and finance ensures that Cadence maintains robust financial health and supports its strategic initiatives.

Wall's professional journey at Cadence began in 1997, and he has held various key positions, including Corporate VP & Corporate Controller and VP Finance & Operations. His extensive experience in finance and operations, coupled with his ACCA qualification from BPP and a Business Studies degree from Munster Technological University, equips him with the skills necessary to lead complex financial projects.

Under Wall's leadership, Cadence has seen significant improvements in financial performance and operational efficiency. His strategic insights and dedication to excellence have been instrumental in driving the company's growth and ensuring its competitive edge in the tech industry.

3) Tom Beckley

Tom Beckley serves as the Senior Vice President and General Manager of the Custom IC & PCB Group at Cadence Design Systems, where he oversees custom design product development and engineering. His leadership ensures the delivery of innovative solutions that meet the evolving needs of the tech industry.

Beckley's career spans several decades, with significant roles at Neolinear, Avant! Corporation, Xynetix Design Systems, Harris Corporation, and General Motors. His extensive experience in mixed signal design, automated testing, and advanced IC packaging has been instrumental in driving Cadence's success.

At Cadence, Beckley manages a team dedicated to pushing the boundaries of custom IC and PCB design. His strategic vision and expertise in financial and technology strategy have been pivotal in advancing the company's initiatives and maintaining its competitive edge.

4) Paul Cunningham

Paul Cunningham serves as the Senior Vice President and General Manager of the System Verification Group at Cadence Design Systems, where he is responsible for overseeing system verification product development and engineering. His role involves ensuring the delivery of cutting-edge verification solutions that meet the industry's evolving demands.

With a professional background that includes key positions at Cadence since 2011 and a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Cambridge, Cunningham brings a wealth of expertise to his role. Notably, he co-founded Azuro, Inc., where he served as CEO, demonstrating his leadership and innovative capabilities in the tech industry.

5) Tina Jones

Tina Jones serves as the Senior Vice President of Global Human Resources at Cadence Design Systems, where she is responsible for overseeing the company's HR strategies and initiatives. Her leadership ensures that Cadence attracts, retains, and develops top talent, fostering a culture of innovation and collaboration.

Jones's extensive career includes roles such as Vice President & Associate General Counsel at Cadence and Associate at Gibson, Dunn & Crutcher LLP. With a B.A. in Political Science from Stanford University and a J.D. from UCLA School of Law, her expertise in legal and human resources has been instrumental in driving key HR projects and initiatives at Cadence.

6) Nimish Modi

Nimish Modi serves as the Senior Vice President and General Manager of Strategy and New Ventures at Cadence Design Systems. In this role, he is responsible for driving strategic initiatives and exploring new business opportunities to ensure the company's growth and innovation.
