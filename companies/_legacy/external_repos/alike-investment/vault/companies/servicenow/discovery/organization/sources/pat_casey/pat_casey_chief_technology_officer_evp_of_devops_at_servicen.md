Pat Casey serves as the Chief Technology Officer and Executive Vice President of DevOps at ServiceNow, a position held since February 2018, where there is oversight of global technology operations. Prior roles at ServiceNow include Senior Vice President of DevOps and Global Head of Engineering, as well as Vice President of Platform Engineering. Pat Casey's career at ServiceNow began in March 2004, progressing through various leadership positions, including Principal Architect and Director of Platform Engineering. Earlier experience includes roles at Peregrine Systems as a Product Author. Pat Casey holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Neurobiology and Neurosciences from Harvey Mudd College, earned between 1990 and 1994.
