## Key Investment Theses from Substack

### Bull Case
- Blinkit acquisition was transformational: $568M → $13B value creation
- Quick commerce TAM expanding rapidly in India ($35B by 2030)
- First-mover advantage with ~46% market share
- Revenue diversification: food delivery + quick commerce + hyperpure + district

### Bear Case
- Valuation stretched beyond fundamentals (P/E near 1,000x)
- Capital-intensive dark store expansion model
- Intense competition from well-funded rivals (Swiggy, Zepto)
- Profitability remains elusive at consolidated level
- Founder departure creates leadership uncertainty
