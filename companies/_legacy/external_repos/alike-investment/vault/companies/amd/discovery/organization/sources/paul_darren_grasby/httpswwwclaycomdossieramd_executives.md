The leadership team at AMD is a powerhouse of strategic vision and decision-making prowess. They set the company's direction with a clear focus on aligning every move with AMD's mission, ensuring that each decision propels the company forward.

Bringing together expertise from diverse fields, this team fosters a culture of innovation and growth. Their collaborative approach drives operational excellence, making AMD a leader in the tech industry.

1) Mark Papermaster

Mark Papermaster serves as the Executive Vice President and Chief Technology Officer at AMD, where he oversees the company's technology direction and engineering operations. His role is pivotal in driving AMD's innovation and maintaining its competitive edge in the tech industry.

Since joining AMD in 2011, Papermaster has significantly contributed to the company's technological advancements, including the development of high-performance computing solutions. He has been instrumental in leading initiatives that enhance AMD's product portfolio and market presence.

With a robust background that includes leadership roles at IBM, Apple Inc., and Cisco, Papermaster brings a wealth of experience in silicon engineering and hardware development. His educational foundation in Electrical and Computer Engineering from The University of Texas at Austin and a Master's degree from the University of Vermont further bolster his expertise. For more details on his career, visit Mark Papermaster's profile.

2) Lisa Su

Lisa Su serves as the Chair and Chief Executive Officer at AMD, where she is responsible for the overall strategic direction and operational execution of the company. Her expertise lies in semiconductor technology and high-performance computing, driving AMD's growth and innovation.

Su's professional journey includes pivotal roles at Freescale Semiconductor and IBM, where she honed her skills in semiconductor R&D and networking. At AMD, she has led significant projects that have expanded the company's product portfolio and market share.

Under Su's leadership, AMD has seen remarkable advancements in technology and market presence. Her impact on the company is profound, steering AMD towards new heights in the tech industry. For more details on her career, visit Lisa Su's profile.

3) Ruth Cotter

Ruth Cotter serves as the Senior Vice President of Marketing, Communications, and Human Resources at AMD, where she plays a crucial role in shaping the company's brand and internal culture. Her responsibilities include overseeing global marketing strategies, corporate communications, and human resources initiatives.

With a career at AMD spanning over two decades, Cotter has held various leadership roles, significantly contributing to investor relations and corporate communications. Her extensive experience in these areas has been instrumental in enhancing AMD's market presence and investor confidence.

Cotter manages a diverse team that drives AMD's marketing and communication efforts, ensuring alignment with the company's strategic goals. She is also involved in key initiatives that foster employee engagement and organizational growth.

4) Robert Gama

Robert Gama serves as the Senior Vice President and Chief Human Resources Officer at AMD, where he is responsible for overseeing the company's human resources strategies and initiatives. His areas of expertise include talent management, organizational development, and employee engagement.

Gama's professional background includes significant roles at Lenovo, PepsiCo, and Dell, where he honed his skills in HR leadership. At AMD, he leads a team dedicated to fostering a positive workplace culture and driving key HR projects that support the company's growth and innovation.

5) Andrej Zdravkovic

Andrej Zdravkovic serves as the Senior Vice President, GPU Technologies and Engineering Software, and Chief Software Officer at AMD. In this role, he oversees the development and integration of software solutions that enhance AMD's GPU technologies, driving innovation and performance improvements.

With a career spanning over two decades at AMD and other tech companies, Zdravkovic has held various leadership positions, including Senior Vice President and Corporate Vice President of Software. His expertise in software development and engineering has been instrumental in advancing AMD's product capabilities and market presence.

6) Jean Hu

Jean Hu serves as the Executive Vice President, Chief Financial Officer, and Treasurer at AMD, where she oversees the company's financial strategy, planning, and operations. Her expertise lies in corporate finance, strategic planning, and business development, ensuring AMD's financial health and growth.

Hu's career includes significant roles at Marvell Semiconductor, QLogic, and Conexant, where she honed her skills in financial leadership and corporate development. She has been instrumental in driving key financial initiatives and projects that support AMD's strategic goals and market expansion.

7) Mathew Hein

Mathew Hein serves as the Senior Vice President and Chief Strategy Officer, Corporate Development at AMD, where he focuses on strategic planning and corporate growth initiatives. His expertise lies in business development, financial analysis, and corporate strategy.
