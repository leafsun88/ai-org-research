Who is the CIO of AMD?

With a career spanning over two decades, Hasmukh Ranjan has established himself as a seasoned IT leader. Currently serving as the Chief Information Officer (CIO) at Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. (AMD), Ranjan brings a wealth of experience from his previous roles at Xilinx and Synopsys. At Xilinx, he managed all facets of IT, including strategy, applications, infrastructure, and information security. His tenure at Synopsys was marked by the integration of IT systems for over 90 mergers and the development of a high-performance computing environment that ranked among the top 500 supercomputers globally. 

Now at AMD, Ranjan is responsible for leading the global IT organization, overseeing corporate IT infrastructure, and delivering advanced solutions for secure and productive business operations.

Work History

Hasmukh Ranjan's work history includes a series of significant roles at leading technology companies. Here is a detailed list of his previous positions:

AMD: SVP & CIO: February 2022 - Present
Xilinx: SVP & CIO: July 2020 - February 2022
Synopsys Inc: CVP & CIO: August 1997 - June 2020
Education History

Hasmukh Ranjan's educational journey is marked by a series of prestigious institutions and diverse fields of study. He began his academic career at Sainik School, Tilaiya, where he earned his High School Diploma from 1979 to 1986. Following this, he pursued a Bachelor of Engineering in Electrical & Electronics at BIT Mesra Student-Industry Relations Cell, completing his degree between 1986 and 1990. Ranjan then advanced his technical expertise with a Master of Science in Computer Science from Northeastern University, which he attended from 1995 to 1997. To round out his education, he obtained an MBA in Management from Northwestern University's Kellogg School of Management, studying there from 2001 to 2003.

When did Hasmukh Ranjan join AMD?

Hasmukh Ranjan joined Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. (AMD) on April 18, 2022, as the Senior Vice President and Chief Information Officer (CIO). With his extensive background in IT management, Ranjan has been instrumental in leading AMD's global IT organization, overseeing corporate IT infrastructure, and delivering advanced solutions for secure and productive business operations. As of October 2023, he has been with AMD for approximately 1 year and 6 months.
