## 4. Competitive Landscape

- Anti-Amazon model: empowering D2C brands vs marketplace
- Merchant Solutions: 73% of revenue (up from minority), driven by Shop Pay
- Shop Pay GMV: +62% YoY — payments becoming major revenue engine
- Global e-commerce projected $8.1T by 2026
- Key competitors: Amazon, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Wix, Squarespace
- Competitive advantage: ecosystem lock-in, developer community, merchant tools

## 5. Bull/Bear Case

**Bull:**
- 30%+ revenue growth with expanding margins — rare combination
- Merchant Solutions flywheel (payments, capital, fulfillment)
- AI-native transformation could drive massive efficiency gains
- Global e-commerce TAM expansion
- Tobi Lutke as founder-CEO advantage

**Bear:**
- P/E ~119x, PEG ~9.6 — extreme valuation demands perfection
- Shopify Capital ($1.73B lending) = credit cycle risk
- Ongoing layoffs + culture erosion may hurt talent attraction
- Tariff/trade uncertainty for international merchants
- 25% YTD stock decline in 2026 despite strong results
