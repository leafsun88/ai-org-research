## Organizational Philosophy (Tobi Lutke)

### "Main Quest" Focus
- 2023: Sold logistics arm (Deliverr) — called it a "side quest"
- Refocused entire company on core merchant platform
- Signal: Founder willing to make painful cuts to maintain focus

### "Shopify OS" — Internal Operating System
- Created configurable system for org management
- Transparent inputs: direct reports, titles, compensation data
- Simulates desired vs actual state
- HR as "reconciler" not "controller"

### Employee Agency
- Choose own compensation split (salary, RSUs, stock, shop cash)
- Rebalanced quarterly
- "Spiky" people valued — mastery paths, not just management track

### AI-First Culture
- AI competency = formal performance review criterion
- Must prove AI can't do job before requesting headcount
- "Reflexive AI usage is a baseline expectation"

## Organizational Structure
- Flat-ish with founder at top
- Decentralized product teams
- High employee agency but top-down strategic direction from Tobi
- "Trust battery" concept: trust builds/depletes through interactions

## Key Observations
- **Strength**: Founder-CEO with deep technical skills and organizational design consciousness
- **Strength**: Willingness to "subtract" (sell logistics, reduce headcount)
- **Risk**: Executive churn (COO, CRO departures) = instability at C-suite
- **Risk**: Culture deterioration from layoffs contradicts "trust battery" philosophy
- **Risk**: AI mandate may create fear-based culture rather than innovation culture
