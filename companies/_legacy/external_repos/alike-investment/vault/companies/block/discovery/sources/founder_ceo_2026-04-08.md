## Bitcoin & Cash App Strategy

### Core Philosophy
- Jack Dorsey left Twitter to focus energy on Bitcoin products
- Long-standing thesis: open protocols should power everyday payments
- Wants Bitcoin to be used as "everyday money"
- Block is "focused on making Bitcoin more accessible and secure for people"

### Cash App Bitcoin Features (2025-2026)
- **Bitcoin direct deposit**: Wages paid out in Bitcoin with no fees or spreads
- **Round-up purchases**: Automatically deposit difference into Bitcoin
- **Bitcoin Map**: Find nearby merchants accepting Bitcoin via Square
- **One-tap conversion**: Convert USD to Bitcoin with single tap
- **Lower fees**: No fees or spreads for Bitcoin purchases over $2,000
- **Higher withdrawal limits**: Increased Bitcoin withdrawal caps

### Square-Cash App Integration
- Rebuilt operating model to tie Cash App more closely to Square
- First bundled release (Nov 2025): 11 new main features, ~150 enhancements
- Stores using Square can auto-convert portion of daily sales to crypto
- Square terminals accept Bitcoin payments; merchants can choose fiat settlement

### Stablecoin Pivot (2025-2026)
- Cash App to launch P2P stablecoin payments in 2026
- Specifically USDC on Solana blockchain
- Cross-border compatibility, low fees, near-instant settlement
- Users can send funds to any digital wallet regardless of jurisdiction
- Represents pragmatic departure from pure Bitcoin maximalism
