## 4. Hindenburg Short Seller Controversy

### The Attack (March 2023)
- Hindenburg disclosed short position in Block
- Shares dropped 15% on disclosure day
- Allegations: exaggerated user counts, failure to curb fraud/illegal activity on Cash App, permitting impersonation

### Block's Response
- Called report "factually inaccurate and misleading"
- Stated intention to work with SEC and explore legal action against Hindenburg
- Claimed report was "designed to deceive and confuse investors"

### Resolution
- Block survived the short attack; stock has recovered significantly
- Hindenburg Research shut down entirely in January 2025
- Founder Nate Anderson cited "wear and tear" of activist short-selling
