# Sensor Tower: State of Gaming 2026 — Mintegral Market Share Data

## Ad Network Market Share (2025)

Among ad networks:
- AdMob leads with 33% (down from 2024)
- AppLovin captures 27.3% of all spend (growing)
- Mintegral at 16% — the strongest growth versus 2024
- Unity and Vungle losing positions slightly

## Social Channel Distribution

- Meta maintains dominance: Instagram 35.6%, Facebook 33.6%
- TikTok decreased to 12.7%, nearly half of last year's share
- YouTube also declined to 12%

## Creative Format Trends

- Video creatives reached 53.7% of ads (up from 44.9%)
- Playables nearly doubled to 13.3%
- Rewarded ads expanded significantly to 17.7% of all impressions (+53.9% YoY)

## Mobile Market Revenue

- Mobile game in-app purchase revenue reached $81.75 billion in 2025 (+1.3% YoY)
- Three consecutive years of expansion
- Downloads continued declining at 7.2% to 50.41 billion installs

## Genre-Specific Dynamics

In the Lifestyle and Puzzle segments, marketing ad spend share exceeds the IAP revenue share in almost every market, indicating intense competitive pressure in these categories.

## Key Implication for Mintegral/Mobvista

Mintegral showed the strongest growth among all ad networks in 2025, capturing 16% market share. This positions Mobvista's core platform as the fastest-growing major ad network, gaining ground on AdMob and AppLovin.
