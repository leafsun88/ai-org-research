# Mintegral: Media Buying in Mobile Games in H1'24 in the US

## Key Findings on Global Gaming Ad Market

The United States leads globally in gaming advertiser count and ranks second in ad impressions. Southeast Asia demonstrates strong competition, placing second in advertiser numbers while leading in total ad views.

## US Market Advertiser Growth

Advertiser expansion in the American market has slowed considerably, with only a 0.25% increase since the previous year. The proportion of newly entering advertisers dropped significantly by 29%, suggesting market saturation and consolidation among established players.

## Creative Content Development

Ad creative production grew modestly by 0.46% year-over-year. Fresh creative assets represented a smaller share, declining by 12%, indicating advertisers are reusing existing content more frequently.

## Game Genre Performance by Ad Views

Puzzle, match-3, and simulation titles dominate impressions. Emerging strength appears in sports gaming (+6 positions), shooter titles (+4 positions), and RPGs (+3 positions), reflecting shifting player interest and advertiser focus.

## Advertisement Format Preferences

Video advertisements command the largest share of the US market. Playable ad formats show expanding adoption, particularly in hyper-casual games (13%), shooters (9%), and action titles (8%).

## Playable Ad Growth Trajectory

Interactive playable advertisements expanded from 3% market penetration in H1'23 to 7% in H1'24, representing substantial momentum in this emerging format category.

## Campaign Duration Patterns

Sports games sustain the longest campaigns (47 days), followed by social titles (41.9 days) and racing games (39 days). Strategy games show shortest durations (24.1 days), with RPGs close behind (23.6 days).
