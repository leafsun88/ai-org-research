# Weekly Gaming Reports Recap: March 9 - March 27 (2026)

## Ad Networks Performance

Among advertising platforms:
- AdMob led with 33% market share in 2025, declining from prior year levels
- AppLovin captured 27.3% of spending and showed growth momentum
- Mintegral achieved 16% share with the strongest growth versus 2024
- Unity and Vungle lost ground slightly

## Mobile Market Revenue

- Mobile game in-app purchase revenue reached $81.75 billion in 2025 (+1.3% YoY)
- Three consecutive years of expansion
- Downloads continued declining at 7.2% to 50.41 billion installs, with deceleration accelerating versus 2024

## Regional and Genre Trends

- Strategy and puzzle games drove strongest revenue growth on mobile
- Asia-based studios grew IAP revenue by $2.58 billion, primarily through Century Games and FUNFLY
- Tencent remained the dominant publisher, with Century Games surging into third place globally

## Creative Format Trends

- Video creatives comprised 53.7% of mobile advertising (up from 44.9%)
- Playables nearly doubled
- Rewarded ads expanded significantly

## Platform Performance

- Nintendo Switch 2 showed exceptional momentum: 17 million units shipped in first six months
- Steam revenue grew 13% to $11.7 billion, outpacing mobile platform growth
- Games received 52 billion downloads globally in 2025, with mobile accounting for 96%

## Key Takeaway for Mintegral/Mobvista

Mintegral continues to be the fastest-growing major ad network, with 16% market share and the strongest growth trajectory among all competitors in 2025 data.
