# Tenjin & GameAnalytics: Hypercasual Games Benchmarks in Q4 2022

## Marketing Metrics

iOS accounted for 48% of marketing spending in Q4 2022, up 5% from the previous quarter, while Android represented 52%. Median CPI reached record highs: $0.42 on iOS and $0.20 on Android.

### Android Top Spenders (by CPI)
- United States: $0.58
- Brazil: $0.07
- Japan: $0.45
- South Korea: $0.33
- UK: $0.30

### iOS Top Spenders (by CPI)
- US: $0.65
- Japan: $0.61
- UK: $0.39
- Canada: $0.49
- France: $0.33

## Ad Network Performance

### Android
Top networks: AppLovin, Google Ads, Mintegral, Meta, ironSource.
- Mintegral demonstrated the lowest median CPI at $0.12
- Snapchat showed the highest at $0.60

### iOS
Top networks: AppLovin, Mintegral, ironSource, TikTok, Google Ads.
- Mintegral maintained the lowest iOS CPI ($0.34)
- Apple Search Ads had the highest ($0.70)

## Product Retention Metrics

### Top-2% Hypercasual Games
- Day-one retention: 45% (iOS), 38% (Android)
- Day-seven retention: 19% (iOS), 14% (Android)

### Top-25% Games
- Day-one retention: 33% (iOS), 28% (Android)
- Day-seven retention: 10% (iOS), 6% (Android)

### Median (All Games)
- Day-one retention: 24% (iOS), 23% (Android)
- Day-seven retention: 7% (iOS), 4% (Android)

## Mintegral Position Summary

Mintegral consistently delivered the lowest CPI across both iOS and Android platforms in the hypercasual gaming segment, making it the most cost-efficient ad network for user acquisition in this category.
