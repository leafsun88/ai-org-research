## Mintegral Strategy & AI Focus

### Programmatic Advertising Strategy (2025-2026)
- AI-powered smart bidding is the core growth engine (>80% of Mintegral revenue)
- Four consecutive smart bidding solutions launched since May 2023
- New products: HybridROAS and IAP ROAS bidding strategies
- Optimizes for advertiser KPIs: ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) and CPE (Cost Per Engagement)

### Financial Execution Under Duan's Leadership
- FY2025 revenue: $2.05B (+35.7% YoY) — crossed $2B for first time
- H1 2025 revenue: $9.38B (likely HKD), +47% YoY
- Net profit: $0.32B, +3.4x YoY
- Mintegral = 95.8% of total revenue — extreme concentration on core product

### Product Portfolio
- **Mintegral**: Programmatic advertising platform (core)
- **GameAnalytics**: SaaS game analytics (acquired 2016)
- **Nativex**: Performance marketing
- **Mindworks/Playturbo**: Creative automation
- **XMP**: Multi-channel ad management tool (Meta Adtech badge partner)
- **AdsPolar**: E-commerce ad management
- **Insightrackr**: Competitive intelligence

### Growth Strategy
- Cloud infrastructure optimization (AWS, Azure, GCP, Huawei Cloud, Alibaba Cloud)
- AI/ML model training investment
- Expanding beyond gaming to non-gaming verticals
- Non-gaming revenue: $115.1M in Q1 2025 (+42.5% YoY)
