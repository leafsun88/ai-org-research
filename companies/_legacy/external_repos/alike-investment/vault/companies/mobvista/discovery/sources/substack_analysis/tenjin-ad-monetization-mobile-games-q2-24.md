# Tenjin: Ad Monetization in Mobile Games in Q2'24

## General Data

- Android captured 63% of ad revenue in Q2'24, up from 59% in Q1'24
- iOS US ad revenue represented 55% of total volume
- Non-top-10 Android countries generated 29% of total ad traffic
- Japan, US, and Australia lead eCPM across iOS formats
- US dominates eCPM on Android across all formats

## iOS Ad Network Distribution

- AppLovin: 35%
- Mintegral: 17%
- Google AdMob: 16%
- Unity Ads: 12%
- ironSource: 8%
- Others: 12%

### iOS eCPM by Format
- Banner: US ($0.99), Russia ($0.73), Canada ($0.70)
- Interstitial: US ($23.17), South Korea ($17.80), Australia ($16.54)
- Rewarded: US ($24.39), South Korea ($20.94), Australia ($19.91)

## Android Ad Network Distribution

- Google AdMob: 26%
- AppLovin: 17%
- Mintegral: 16%
- Unity Ads: 13%
- Liftoff: 5%
- Others: 23%

### Android eCPM by Format
- Banner: Canada ($2.00), US ($1.18), Japan ($0.86)
- Interstitial: US ($16.15), Canada ($12.65), Japan ($11.35)
- Rewarded: US ($30.25), Canada ($23.13), South Korea ($22.01)

## Top Revenue Markets
- iOS: US (52%), Japan (10%), Russia (8%)
- Android: US (30%), Russia (14%), Brazil (6%)

## Mintegral Position Summary

Mintegral holds the #2 position on iOS (17%) and #3 on Android (16%) among ad networks in mobile gaming monetization, trailing only AppLovin and Google AdMob respectively.
