Chief Strategy Officer, Co-founder

Copied

Brian Venturo is one of CoreWeave’s co-founders and has served as a member of the board of directors since April 2019 and as Chief Strategy Officer since March 2024. From October 2017 to March 2024, Mr. Venturo served as Chief Technology Officer. Previously, from January 2013 to January 2018, Mr. Venturo was a Partner at Hudson Ridge Asset Management LLC, a natural gas hedge fund. He previously served as Portfolio Manager - Energy and Emissions for the asset management and advisory firm Natsource Asset Management LLC (May 2007–December 2012). Mr. Venturo earned a B.A. in Economics from Haverford College.
