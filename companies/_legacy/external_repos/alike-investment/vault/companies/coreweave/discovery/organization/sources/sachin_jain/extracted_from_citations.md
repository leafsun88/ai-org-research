Sachin Jain

COO

CoreWeave

Sachin Jain is Chief Operating Officer at CoreWeave, where he leads global operations including supply chain, procurement, capacity, data center operations, and international expansion. He plays a key role in scaling the company to support demanding AI workloads. Previously, he was Senior Vice President for AI Infrastructure, Data Center Supply, and IaaS Products at Oracle Cloud. Before that, he led capacity planning, data center software, and supply-chain engineering teams at Google Cloud. Earlier in his career, he served as Vice President at Amazon, overseeing multiple business units across pricing, retail, seller services, and AWS.
