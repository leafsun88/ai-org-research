Profile summary

Share this profile

Sandy Venugopal is an accomplished technology executive with over 20 years of experience in IT leadership and digital transformation. She is the Chief Information Officer (CIO) at CoreWeave – a fast-growing AI cloud infrastructure company – where she leads the company’s IT strategy and digital transformation initiatives. 

Previously, she served as CIO at cybersecurity firm SentinelOne and Uber Technologies and held key leadership roles at LinkedIn. Venugopal is recognized for her ability to scale global operations, drive enterprise innovation, and foster cross-functional collaboration. She also serves on the Board of Directors at The Adecco Group.

Claim to fame

AI-driven security at SentinelOne

Sandy led investments in generative AI to automate threat detection and response. By leveraging internal logs and predictive models, her team empowered SOC analysts with actionable insights. This helped reduce mean time to detect and improve incident triage.

Facilitating AI enterprise knowledge discovery

She deployed AI search and knowledge discovery tool, Glean, at Uber and SentinelOne to enhance enterprise search, streamlining knowledge discovery and collaboration. These deployments improved information accessibility and boosted employee productivity across departments.

Scaling IT at Uber

During Uber’s hyper-growth phase, Sandy scaled IT infrastructure and tools globally, ensuring operational excellence across regions. She implemented automation and self-service solutions to support rapid onboarding and global expansion.
