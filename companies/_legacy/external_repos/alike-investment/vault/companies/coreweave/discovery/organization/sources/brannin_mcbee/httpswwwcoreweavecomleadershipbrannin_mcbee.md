Copied

Brannin McBee is one of CoreWeave’s co-founders and has served as Chief Development Officer since March 2024. From September 2017 to March 2024, Mr. McBee served as Chief Strategy Officer. He was a Proprietary Trader at Active Power Investments, focused on North American Natural Gas, Power, and Agriculture markets, from April 2020 to January 2021. Previously, Mr. McBee was Vice President at Fourth Floor Coastal LLC (March 2017–August 2018) and a Proprietary Trader at Windy Bay Power LLC (January 2013–January 2018). Mr. McBee earned a B.S. in Finance from the University of Colorado Boulder.
