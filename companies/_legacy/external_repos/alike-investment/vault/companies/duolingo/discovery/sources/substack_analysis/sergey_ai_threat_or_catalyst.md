# Duolingo: Is AI Really a Threat, or Its Biggest Growth Catalyst? — Sergey

**Source**: https://sergeycyw.substack.com/p/duolingo-is-ai-really-a-threat-or

## Key Thesis
Examines the central debate around Duolingo: whether AI/LLMs represent an existential threat to the language learning platform or its biggest growth catalyst.

## Arguments for AI as Catalyst
- Duolingo has partnered with OpenAI to integrate GPT-4 into its platform
- AI enables personalized tutoring at scale (Explain My Answer, Roleplay features)
- Proprietary user data + AI = potential superhuman tutor
- Since ChatGPT launched, DAU grew from 15M to 50M — AI hasn't cannibalized growth

## Arguments for AI as Threat
- GPT-5 and other LLMs can provide free, personalized language instruction
- Duolingo Max (AI-powered tier) has only 0.4% adoption
- The entire category of structured language learning could be compressed by AI
