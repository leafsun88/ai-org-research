# Luis von Ahn - Co-Founder, CEO & Chairman

## Identity
- **Full Name:** Luis von Ahn, Ph.D.
- **Born:** August 19, 1978, Guatemala City, Guatemala
- **Nationality:** Guatemalan-American (German descent on father's side)
- **Education:**
  - B.S. Mathematics, Duke University (2000)
  - Ph.D. Computer Science, Carnegie Mellon University (2005)

## Career Trajectory
1. **Carnegie Mellon University** (2005-2011) - Professor, School of Computer Science
   - Invented CAPTCHA and reCAPTCHA (human computation / crowdsourcing pioneer)
   - Sold reCAPTCHA to Google in 2009
   - MacArthur Fellow at age 28 (2006)
2. **Duolingo** (2011-present) - Co-Founder & CEO
   - Co-founded with PhD student Severin Hacker
   - Led company from concept to IPO (NASDAQ, Jul 2021)
   - Chairman of the Board with dual-class voting control

## Key Findings
- **Inventor-CEO archetype**: Rare combination of deep academic CS credentials (human computation pioneer) and successful consumer product scaling
- **Mission-driven**: Founded Duolingo to provide free language education, inspired by limited educational access in Guatemala
- **Social impact**: Established Luis von Ahn Foundation (2021) supporting Guatemalan women and girls
- **Recognition**: MacArthur Fellow (2006), Lemelson-MIT Prize $500K (2018), National Inventors Hall of Fame inductee
- **Dual-class control**: Maintains voting control of the company through Class B shares
