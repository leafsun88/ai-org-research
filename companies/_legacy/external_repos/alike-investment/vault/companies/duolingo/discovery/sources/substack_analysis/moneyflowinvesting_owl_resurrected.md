**Source**: https://moneyflowinvesting.substack.com/p/duolingo-duol-the-owl-was-resurrected

## Key Thesis (Bearish)
A contrarian/bearish view arguing that a smart investment demands asymmetry — a price where the upside is probable and the downside is protected — but Duolingo offers neither, leaving investors paying a blue-chip premium for what is effectively a long-shot venture-style turnaround.

## Bear Case Arguments

- Management's pivot to user growth over monetization creates execution risk
- AI disruption remains a material threat to the core business model
