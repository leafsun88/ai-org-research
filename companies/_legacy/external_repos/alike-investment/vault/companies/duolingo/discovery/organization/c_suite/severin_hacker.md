# Severin Hacker - Co-Founder & CTO

## Identity
- **Full Name:** Severin Hacker
- **Born:** July 6, 1984, Zug, Switzerland
- **Nationality:** Swiss-American
- **Education:**
  - B.S. Computer Science, ETH Zurich (2006)
  - Ph.D. Computer Science, Carnegie Mellon University (2014)

## Career Trajectory
1. **ETH Zurich** - Undergraduate studies in Computer Science
2. **Carnegie Mellon University** (2006-2014) - PhD student under Luis von Ahn
   - Doctoral research on language translation through crowdsourcing led to Duolingo concept
3. **Duolingo** (2009/2011-present) - Co-Founder & CTO
   - Built original ML-powered adaptive learning platform
   - Leads all technology and engineering

## Key Findings
- **Technical co-founder still active**: Rare in public companies at this scale; signals strong technical DNA in leadership
- **Self-taught programmer**: Started coding at age 12 driven by video game interest
- **ML-first from day one**: Pioneered using machine learning to personalize language learning and predict forgetting patterns
- **Awards**: Crunchie Award for Best Startup (2014), MIT Technology Review "Top Innovators under 35" (2014), One Young World Entrepreneur of the Year (2019)
- **Mission focus**: Originally wanted Duolingo to be "100% free" for the most disadvantaged users
