## Organizational Observations

### Founder-Led Structure
Both co-founders remain in active executive roles (CEO + CTO). Luis von Ahn holds dual-class voting control as Chairman. This is a strong founder-led company with aligned incentives.

### Key Talent Patterns
1. **Deep tech + consumer DNA**: Multiple execs from Google (Meese, Kansu), gaming (Dunaway from Nintendo, Ross from Halo), and design-first companies (Sims from Stripe/Adobe)
2. **Monetization expertise**: Kansu (ex-Google Adwords) and Meese (ex-Google Play) drove the subscription transformation
3. **Mission-aligned board**: Jim Shelton (education), John Lilly (consumer tech), Laela Sturdy (growth investing) bring complementary perspectives
4. **Recent additions signal expansion**: Bonnie Ross (gaming) and Mario Schlosser (healthcare tech/AI) suggest interest in gamification and AI-driven personalization

### CFO Transition (Jan 2026)
Matt Skaruppa's departure after leading the IPO and 6 years of scaling is notable. Gillian Munson's appointment from within the board suggests continuity. Her Vimeo experience (subscription media) is directly relevant.

### Avg Tenure
- C-Suite average: ~5.5 years
- Board average: ~5.8 years (excluding 2024 additions)
