# Jay Kreps — Confluent CEO/Co-founder Profile

## Background
- Co-creator of Apache Kafka at LinkedIn
- Co-founded Confluent with Neha Narkhede and Jun Rao
- Worked on Kafka for ~10 years before founding Confluent
- LinkedIn: currently lists role at Anthropic (note: verify -- may be LinkedIn data artifact)
- Author of "I Heart Logs" (O'Reilly)

## Leadership Philosophy

### "Why" Over "How"
- Kreps emphasizes stepping back to ask "why" rather than just focusing on "how"
- Analyzes complex business and technical requirements holistically
- Develops elegant, flexible designs rather than quick fixes

### Prioritization & Focus
- Believes high-output people focus time on highest-impact things
- When managing multiple projects at LinkedIn, put nearly all time into Kafka (the most important one)
- Philosophy: prioritize important things, cut less important ones to produce more

### Communication & Empathy
- Despite working in deeply complex technical areas, communicates clearly
- Values empathy: tries to put himself in others' shoes
- Focuses on what's on others' minds rather than just his own perspective

### Long-Term Thinking
- Started Kafka 10 years before founding Confluent
- Used that time to deeply understand "what they wanted to do and why it was valuable"
- Patient approach to company-building and product development
