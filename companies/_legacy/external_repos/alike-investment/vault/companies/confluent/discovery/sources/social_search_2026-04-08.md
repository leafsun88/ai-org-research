# Confluent (CFLT) — Social Media & Community Search

## Twitter/X Discussions

### IBM Acquisition ($11B)
- IBM to acquire Confluent for $31/share in cash, enterprise value ~$11B
- Transaction expected to close mid-2026, subject to shareholder and regulatory approval
- Represents ~50% premium to pre-deal trading levels
- Confluent uses X (Twitter), LinkedIn, Facebook for Reg FD disclosure

### Financial Performance Highlights
- Q3 2025: 24% YoY Confluent Cloud revenue growth, 43% YoY RPO growth acceleration
- FY2025 subscription revenue: $1,120M (+21% YoY)
- Confluent Cloud revenue: $624M (+27% YoY)

### Product Innovation
- Confluent announced "Streaming Agents" for event-driven AI agents
- Features: Agent Definition, built-in observability/debugging, Real-Time Context Engine
- Named Leader in Forrester Wave: Streaming Data Platforms Q4 2025 (highest scores in 14 criteria)
