## 3. Competitive Landscape: Confluent vs Redpanda vs Kafka

### Market Position
- Apache Kafka: de facto standard, 100,000+ organizations
- Confluent: broadest adoption, deepest enterprise feature set, most expensive
- Redpanda: rebuilt core for modern infra, gaining traction as cheaper alternative

### User Ratings (Gartner Peer Insights / G2)
- Confluent: 4.6 stars (204 reviews)
- Redpanda: 4.6 stars (22 reviews) -- smaller sample

### Cost Comparison
- Redpanda claims up to 60% cheaper for fan-in, 58% cheaper for fan-out
- Confluent is premium-priced due to comprehensive feature set

### Strategic Differences
- Confluent: extends original Kafka vision + rich cloud platform + governance
- Redpanda: rebuilds Kafka core for performance and simplicity (C++ vs JVM)
- Other competitors: Pulsar, WarpStream, StreamNative, AutoMQ

### Key Competitive Risk
- Kafka protocol is open source -- competitors can leverage it
- Confluent's moat: continuous R&D (22% of revenue), enterprise features, switching costs
