# Axon Stock Analysis — TIKR 2026 Research

*Source: TIKR (Gian Estrada)*

## Key Takeaways

- **UK Deployment Expansion:** January 2025 rollout of 160 body-worn cameras across 42 UK fire stations (Norfolk Fire partnership)
- **Balance Sheet Action:** Axon redeemed 0.5% convertible notes due 2027 in February 2026 at 100% of principal, simplifying capital structure
- **Price Framework:** Based on 33% revenue growth and a normalized 63x P/E, Axon stock could reach **$644 by December 2027**
- **Return Math:** From current price of $434, the $644 target implies **49% upside** and a **23% annualized return** over ~2 years

## Business Overview

Axon Enterprise provides public safety technology combining body cameras, conducted energy devices, and cloud software sold on multi-year subscriptions. Revenue exceeded $2 billion in 2024.

- **Gross profit:** Reached $1 billion in 2024
- **Gross margin:** ~60%
- **EBITDA margins:** Near 25%
- **Operating income:** ~$80 million (4% GAAP operating margin vs 25% EBITDA — large SBC gap)
- **Normalized net income:** $470 million in 2024

## Valuation Model

**TIKR's valuation model assumptions:**
1. **Revenue Growth:** 32.8% (slightly below historical 33.2% — assumes durability at scale, not acceleration)
2. **Operating Margins:** 4.9% (below historical 9.9% — reflects continued reinvestment in international rollout, software development, platform expansion)
3. **Exit P/E Multiple:** 62.8x (vs current 101.9x — assumes valuation compression as growth matures)

**Model conclusion: Buy signal** — 23.3% annualized return exceeds typical 10% equity hurdle.

## Scenario Analysis (Through 2029)

- **Low Case:** Revenue growth 21.2%, net margins 18.0% → **16.2% annualized return**
- **Mid Case:** Revenue growth 23.5%, margins improving to 20.6% → **27.5% annualized return**
- **High Case:** International adoption accelerates, platform economics scale, revenue 25.8%, margins 23.1% → **38.8% annualized return**

## Recent Positive Catalyst

- **January 2025:** Partnership with Norfolk Fire deploying 160 Axon Body 4 cameras across 42 UK fire stations. Senior Director Alex Lowe stated, "Our cameras have been invaluable for first responders." This extends adoption beyond policing into fire services.
- **February 2026:** Redemption of 0.5% convertible notes reduces dilution risk, reinforcing the earnings path.

## Key Risks

- Sustaining 32.8% growth requires continued international adoption and higher software attach rates at scale
- Margins compressed toward 4.9% due to expansion, international rollout, and software development costs
- "Limited tolerance for execution slippage" at current premium valuation
