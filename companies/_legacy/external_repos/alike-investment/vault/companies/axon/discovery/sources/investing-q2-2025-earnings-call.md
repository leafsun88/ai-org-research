# Earnings Call Transcript: Axon Enterprise Q2 2025 Earnings Beat Expectations

*Source: Investing.com*

Axon Enterprise Inc. (AXON), market cap $58.12 billion, reported robust Q2 2025 results, exceeding both earnings and revenue forecasts.

## Key Metrics

- **EPS:** $2.12 (vs. $1.46 expected — **+45.21% surprise**)
- **Revenue:** $669 million (vs. $640.3M forecast)
- **Revenue growth:** +33% YoY — **14th consecutive quarter of over 25% revenue growth**
- **Software & Services revenue:** $292M, +39% YoY
- **Connected Devices revenue:** $376M, +29% YoY
  - TASER: +19% (TASER 10)
  - Personal sensors (Axon Body 4): +24%
  - Platform solutions: +86% (counter-drone and VR)
- **Adjusted gross margin:** 63.3% (+20 bps YoY)
- **Adjusted EBITDA margin:** 25.7%
- **Net Revenue Retention:** 124%

## 2025 Guidance (Raised)

- **Full-year revenue:** $2.65B to $2.73B (~29% growth at midpoint)
- **Adjusted EBITDA:** $665M to $685M

## CEO Rick Smith Key Remarks

> "We have so many horses that can run here that we're not dependent on any one part of the business."

> "Collaboration in public safety isn't just critical anymore. It is absolutely essential."

On AI adoption: "Draft one remains our fastest adopted software solution. TASER 10, our fastest adopted TASER weapon. Axon Body four, our fastest adopted camera."

On AI investment: "I am so proud of our sales and product teams... you are truly seeing what a world class team is capable of doing when everybody is on the same mission."

On international Europe (from extensive summer meetings): "I had my first ever meeting with a prime minister of a NATO nation. Last week, I met with several ministers of the interior level folks... There is a ton of need." European border agencies requesting TASER 10 tuning for handling multiple people simultaneously.

On counter-drone: "Ukraine has taken out a sizable percentage of Russia's strategic bomber fleet with thousand dollar FPV drones... everybody's attention has been grabbed whether you run a stadium or a nuclear reactor or a power station or responsible for protecting the executive's home."

## CFO Brittany Bagley Key Remarks

- Net Revenue Retention at **124% — near or above 120 for twenty consecutive quarters**
- Platform solutions grew 86%: "Every category in that segment is growing really nicely." Noted "lumpiness" expected in counter-drone segment due to large deals.
- Tariff impact: "Adjusted EBITDA margin... benefiting from the timing of tariffs, which will now impact us more in the second half of the year."

## Sales President Josh Key Remarks

On record bookings: "This team took back the record for the largest deal in Axon company history by a wide margin. This contract with a major city police department also marked the largest contract in terms of new product bookings encompassing everything from drones to our AI products."

On AI Era Plan: **$150 million of AI Era Plan bookings in Q2 alone**. "Over 30% of bookings this quarter came from new product categories."

On per-officer revenue expansion: "A few years ago, the maximum an agency could spend with us on a per officer basis was less than $300. Today, that sits over $600 due to new products."

On H2 outlook: "We now have line of sight to deliver year over year bookings growth in the high 30% range."

Potential federal opportunity in counter-drone, FUSUS video products, drone technology, potentially TASER. Management mapping specific customers.

"The pipeline in the back half of this year for the AI era plan is loaded... customers are super pleased with Draft One... now with AI assistant and real-time translator and FormOne and BriefOne getting going, there is a lot of customer excitement."

**On fiber-optic drones:**
Smith: "Russia and Ukraine... unjammable because they're flying with a physical connection. We're digging deep on how do we extend our solution set to cover this thing."
