# Axon Reports Q4 2025 Revenue of $797 Million, Up 39% Year-Over-Year

*Source: PR Newswire (Official Axon Press Release), February 24, 2026*

## Headline Metrics

- **Q4 2025 Revenue:** $797 million (+39% YoY)
- **Software & Services revenue:** $343 million (+40% YoY)
- **Full-year 2025 Revenue:** $2.8 billion (+33% YoY) — fourth consecutive year of annual growth above 30%
- **Net income:** $3 million (0.3% net income margin)
- **Non-GAAP net income:** $178 million (22.3% non-GAAP net income margin)
- **Adjusted EBITDA:** $206 million (25.9% Adjusted EBITDA margin, +46% YoY)
- **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR):** $1.347 billion (+35% YoY)
- **Net Revenue Retention:** 125% (accelerating)
- **Future contracted bookings:** $14.4 billion (+43% YoY)
- **Full-year bookings:** $7.4 billion (+46% YoY)

## 2025 Full Year Highlights

- Delivered **$2.8 billion in annual revenue**, up 33% YoY
- Surpassed **$1.3 billion in annual recurring revenue**, up 35% YoY
- Generated **annual bookings of $7.4 billion**, up 46% YoY
- Reached **total future contracted bookings of $14.4 billion**, +43% YoY
- Achieved full-year net income margin of 4.5% alongside **25.5% Adjusted EBITDA margin**
- **Balance sheet:** $1.7 billion in cash/investments, $1.8 billion in convertible/senior notes (net debt position of $112M, down $469M sequentially due to acquisitions)

## Major 2025 Product Innovations

**AI Era:**
- **Axon Assistant:** Voice-activated AI companion directly in body cameras. ~500 customers live. Real-time translation across 50+ languages; voice-enabled policy chat.
- **Draft One & Brief One & Form One:** AI report writing suite. Foundational tools reducing administrative burden.

**Axon Vehicle Intelligence:**
- Entered fixed ALPR segment. License plate recognition, live streaming, real-time alerts. Goes beyond traditional ALPR with vehicle descriptors (make, color, visible damage).

**Axon Body Mini (Announced, shipping mid-2026):**
- Compact, durable body-worn camera for commercial environments (retail, healthcare, logistics).

**Axon 911:**
- Modernizing 911 call handling with AI-enabled communications. Acquisitions of **Prepared** (closed late 2025) and **Carbyne** (closed Q1 2026) are driving this new product category.
- First integration connecting Axon 911 and Axon Fusus already shipped.

## Q4 Segment Results

**Software & Services:**
- Revenue: $342.5M, +39.8% YoY, +12.2% QoQ
- Gross margin: 72.9% GAAP (76.7% adjusted)
- Software-only gross margin exceeded 80%

**Connected Devices:**
- Revenue: $454.2M, +37.6% YoY, +12.0% QoQ
- Gross margin: 46.6% GAAP (49.3% adjusted) — down YoY due to tariffs and platform solutions product mix

## 2026 Guidance
