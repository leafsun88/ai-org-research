# AXON Q4 2024 Earnings: Deep Dive into a Public Safety Powerhouse

*Source: Arya Deniz, Substack*

**Axon Enterprise (NASDAQ: AXON)** closed out 2024 with a bang – delivering another quarter of 30%+ growth and showcasing the fruits of its transformation from a TASER stun-gun maker into a full-fledged public safety technology platform.

## 1. Financial Deep Dive

**Blistering Growth with Shifting Revenue Mix:** 2024 marked Axon's third consecutive year of 30%+ revenue growth, with full-year revenue up **33% to $2.1 billion**. In Q4 alone, revenue hit a record $575 million (+34% YoY). Revenue breakdown:

- **TASER** devices: ~**$819 million** (~39% of 2024 revenue, +~30% YoY)
- **Axon Cloud & Services**: **$806 million** (~39% of revenue, +44% YoY)
- **Sensors & Other**: ~**$458 million** (~22% of revenue, +18% YoY)

Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) reached **$1.0 billion (+37% YoY)**, with **95%+ of Axon's business tied to subscription plans**. Dollar-based net revenue retention was **123%** with **"de minimis attrition"** (churn essentially near zero).

**Margins:**
- **Axon Cloud & Services** gross margin: ~**73.7% GAAP** in Q4 (software-only exceeds 80%)
- **TASER segment**: Q4 GAAP gross margin **61.3%**, up 420 bps YoY; adjusted 63.7%, up 610 bps
- **Sensors & Other**: Q4 gross margin **32.8% GAAP** — steep decline from ~47% due to new Axon Body 4 ramp costs and legacy inventory write-offs

**Earnings Quality:**
- GAAP net income: **$377M** (18.1% net margin)
- Non-GAAP net income: **$466M**
- SBC: ~**$325 million** related to 2024 eXponential Stock Plan; 2025 guide $580-630M
- Diluted share count rose ~4% YoY
- Non-GAAP EPS: **$5.94**, up 43% YoY

**Free Cash Flow:** $329 million GAAP ($344M adjusted) — more than double 2023's $148M. FCF margin ~16% with **60%+ free cash flow conversion**.

## 2. Segment & Business Strategy

### TASER Business

**TASER segment revenue jumped 37% YoY in Q4** as agencies adopt the **TASER 10** (10 shots vs 2 in older models, extended 45-foot range). Axon **shipped over 200,000 TASER devices in 2024** — record volume. Also shipped **over 9 million TASER cartridges** — a recurring revenue stream. Selling TASER as a service: combining device, cartridges, training, and upgrades on subscription.

### Axon Cloud: SaaS at Scale

Key products:
- **Axon Evidence:** Digital evidence management — foundational cloud platform
- **Axon Records:** Cloud-based records management system — modern replacement for legacy police incident report systems
- **Axon Respond:** Real-time operations platform with live GPS and video streaming
- **Draft One:** AI report writing tool — fastest-growing software product; contributed **100,000+ incident reports**, saving **2.2 million minutes** of officers' time in 2024
- **AI Era Plan:** Premium bundle for AI features — key upsell opportunity

**Total backlog of future contracted revenue: $10.1B (+42% YoY)**. ~20-25% expected to recognize in next 12 months.

### Sensors & Fleet

- **Axon Body 4:** ~300,000 body cameras shipped. Real-time cellular streaming, enhanced resolution.
- **Axon Fleet 3:** In-car system with integrated ALPR
- **Axon Air & Drones:** Acquired Dedrone (counter-drone); partnered with Skydio for DFR program

### Enterprise Expansion

**Enterprise segment bookings tripled year-over-year**. New **Axon Body 4 Workforce** for commercial use. **Largest deal in company history** from an enterprise customer.

## 3. Bookings & Backlog

- Annual bookings **over $5 billion in 2024**
- **$10.1B backlog (+42% YoY)** — exceptional revenue visibility
- ~80-90% of 2025 revenue already "in the bag" from signed deals

## 4. International Acceleration

International bookings grew **nearly 50% sequentially in Q4** (triple-digit % YoY growth). Key markets: UK, Germany, Australia, EMEA, APAC. International TAM pegged at additional **$32B+** opportunity.

## 5. Competitive Positioning

Key competitors:
- **Motorola Solutions (MSI):** Most significant competitor, but Axon is gaining share across body cameras and cloud
- **Tyler Technologies (TYL):** Legacy on-prem records/RMS systems
- **Mark43:** Private cloud-first competitor for records software
- **VirTra:** Police training simulators (tiny vs Axon)

**M&A potential:** ~$800M in cash at year-end plus $300M+ annual FCF. Possible targets: AI analytics startups, dispatch/911 technology.
