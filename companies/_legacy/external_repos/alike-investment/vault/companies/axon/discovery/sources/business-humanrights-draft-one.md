# USA: A new generative-AI policing tool by Axon Enterprise allegedly built to evade accountability

*Source: Business and Human Rights Resource Centre*

In 2025, Axon released its "Draft One" tool, which uses body-worn camera audio and generative AI to auto-create police-report drafts. Civil-rights analysts argue the system is "allegedly designed to evade accountability for bias or mistakes," citing how the tool can obscure when AI is used and reduce transparency and human oversight.

The concerns include how officers are required to review and correct AI-generated narratives, but audit logs and disclosures about the AI's role remain opaque, raising questions about fairness and error correction in policing.

The Business and Human Rights Resource Centre invited Axon to respond, and their responses are noted in the timeline below.

## Timeline

- **3 Nov 2025** — Axon Enterprise, Inc. Company Response
- **10 Jul 2025** — Article: "USA: Axon's generative AI-powered policing tool allegedly designed to evade accountability for bias or mistakes"

Axon's Draft One AI tool for generating police reports from body-camera audio is allegedly designed, according to researchers at EFF, with minimal oversight and no audit trail, making it nearly impossible to distinguish officer input from AI output or hold either accountable for errors or bias.

## Summary of Concerns

1. **No audit trail:** The system does not store original AI-generated drafts; they disappear when the officer closes the browser tab
2. **Transparency gap:** It is often impossible to tell which parts of a police report were generated by AI and which were written by an officer
3. **Human oversight reduced:** Departments have been turning off oversight features (such as required officer edits and AI-disclosure headers)
4. **Bias risk:** AI algorithms may produce racially or gender-biased outputs, exacerbating existing inequalities in policing
5. **Accountability gap:** When errors appear in reports, there is no way to determine whether the AI or the officer is responsible

*Note: Axon was invited to respond and provided a Company Response dated November 3, 2025.*
