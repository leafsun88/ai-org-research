Who is the CEO of Snowflake?

Sridhar Ramaswamy has had a distinguished career in the tech industry, marked by his contributions to digital advertising, engineering, and venture partnerships. Before joining Snowflake, Ramaswamy co-founded Neeva, a privacy-focused search engine, and served as a Venture Partner at Greylock Partners. His tenure at Google saw him rise to Senior Vice President of Ads & Commerce, where he played a crucial role in scaling Google's advertising business. Ramaswamy's technical and leadership skills have consistently driven innovation and operational excellence in his roles.

Education History

Sridhar Ramaswamy's educational background is rooted in computer science. He earned a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Brown University, where he studied from 1989 to 1994. Prior to that, he completed his Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science at the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras, from 1985 to 1989. This solid foundation in computer science has significantly contributed to his career achievements and leadership roles in the tech industry.

When did Sridhar Ramaswamy join Snowflake?

Sridhar Ramaswamy joined Snowflake in June 2023 initially as part of a learning phase and was appointed Chief Executive Officer on February 1, 2024. His journey within the company is marked by a period of intense learning and strategic planning, culminating in his leadership role. His vision and expertise are expected to drive Snowflake's continued innovation and expansion in the cloud data warehousing space.
