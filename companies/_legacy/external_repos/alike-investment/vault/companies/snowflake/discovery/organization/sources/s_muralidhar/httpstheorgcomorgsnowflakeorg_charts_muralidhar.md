S. “Murali” Muralidhar serves as Snowflake’s Chief Technology Officer and has been with the company since 2017. In his previous roles as Software Engineer and Technical Fellow at Snowflake, he focused on data infrastructure, metadata framework, and product platform areas and served as the key architect reviewing technical designs, strategy, and managing related roadmap development efforts. Before joining Snowflake, Murali worked in leadership roles at Facebook, Microsoft, and Oracle.

Murali holds a BTech in Computer Science from the Indian Institute of Technology in Madras and an MS in Computer Science from the University of Wisconsin, Madison. He resides in Mercer Island, Washington.
