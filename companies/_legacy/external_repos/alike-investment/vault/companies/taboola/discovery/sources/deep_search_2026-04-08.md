## 4. Controversies & Risks (2025-2026)

### Platform Quality Issues
- User reports of unauthorized countries added to campaigns (Albania, Afghanistan)
- Support blamed users; refused to acknowledge bug
- Reported financial losses (~$700 per affected user)

### Revenue Miss Concern
- Q4 2025 revenue missed guidance ($522M vs $532-542M guided)
- Stock dropped ~10.6% post-earnings
- Market questioning near-term growth catalysts

### AI Disruption Risk
- BTIG flagged concern that AI could erode pricing power
- AI-generated content may reduce publisher page views (Taboola's core inventory)
- Google AI Overviews potentially reducing referral traffic to publishers

### Brand Safety / Content Quality
- Historical Infowars ad placement controversy (Taboola pulled ads)
- Ongoing perception issue: "clickbait" recommendation widgets
- Apple News+ integration raised questions about editorial standards

### Concentration Risk
- Yahoo partnership: ~$1B annual revenue = ~52% of total revenue
- 30-year term provides stability but creates dependency
- Yahoo's own traffic trends matter significantly

Source: [Capterra Reviews](https://www.capterra.com/p/156009/Taboola/reviews/), [Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/taboola-com-tbla-down-10-131118290.html), [Digiday](https://digiday.com/media/conspiracy-peddling-sites-microscope-taboola-yanks-content-ads-off-infowars/)

## 5. Yahoo Partnership & AI Products

### Yahoo Partnership (30-Year Deal)
- Exclusive native advertising partner across ALL Yahoo digital properties
- Expected ~$1B annual revenue to Taboola
- Yahoo properties worldwide now available through Taboola platform
- ~900M monthly Yahoo users reached
- Future milestones: technical & data integrations for better audience targeting

### AI Product Portfolio

**Realize Platform (launched Feb 2025)**
- Beyond native advertising — full performance advertising stack
- AI performance engine across Taboola's publisher network
- Partners: TIME, Weather Channel Digital, Gannett/USA TODAY, Nexstar, Slate
- Technologies: Max Conversions, "Abby" AI optimizer
- CEO called it Taboola's "Amazon moment"

**DeeperDive (launched June 2025)**
- Gen AI answer engine living on publisher websites
- Uses publisher's own content (anti-AI-search-cannibalization)
- Design partners: USA TODAY, The Independent, BuzzFeed Asia, Bangkok Post
- Results: ~3x higher revenue per user, more page views, higher return rates
- Positions publishers to compete with AI search engines

**LG Ad Solutions Partnership (Dec 2025)**
- CTV-to-web Performance Enhancer
- Combines LG first-party ACR data with Taboola's Realize platform
- Extends Smart TV campaigns into open web publisher sites
- "First large-scale TV partnership across hundreds of millions of devices"

**Taboola expects to pay >$1.5B to publishers and OEMs in 2025**
