## Strategic Vision & Philosophy

### Core Positioning
- Three pillars of digital advertising: Search (Google), Social (Facebook), Discovery (Taboola)
- "Amazon moment" — Realize platform expanding beyond native into full performance advertising
- Video-first strategy as early differentiator

### AI Strategy
- DeeperDive: Gen AI answer engine for publishers (anti-AI-search-cannibalization)
- Realize: AI performance engine for advertiser campaigns
- Max Conversions and "Abby" AI tools for campaign optimization
- First-party data + AI as competitive moat

### Publisher Relationships
- Close working relationships with publishers as core competitive advantage
- Expects to pay >$1.5B to publishers and OEMs in 2025
- Yahoo 30-year exclusive native advertising partnership (~$1B annual revenue)
- Publisher Product Council to co-develop AI features

### Leadership Style Signals
- Long-term thinking (30-year Yahoo deal, 5 years of zero revenue at founding)
- Product-obsessed (personally driving DeeperDive, Realize, CTV strategy)
- Sees publishers as partners, not commodities
- Frames Taboola as defender of open web against walled gardens
