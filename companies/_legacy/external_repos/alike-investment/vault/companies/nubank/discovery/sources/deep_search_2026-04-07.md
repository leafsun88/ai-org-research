## 4. Risk & Regulation

### Regulatory Risk
- Brazil central bank tightening: new rule requires banking license to use "bank" in branding
- Cybersecurity incidents: Pix intermediary breach in 2025 caused $130M+ losses industry-wide
- Stricter capital requirements, audit requirements imposed on payment institutions
- Currency risk: BRL volatility remains persistent concern

### Credit Risk
- NPL rates 25-30% better than competitors due to proprietary models
- But personal loan book growing rapidly — untested in downturn
- nuFormer AI model deployed for credit underwriting in Brazil

### Macro Risk
- Brazil Selic rate elevated
- LatAm FX exposure (revenue in BRL, MXN, COP)
- Political/regulatory environment in Mexico and Colombia less predictable

### Operational Risk
- U.S. market entry execution — crowded market with established players
- Scaling from 131M to global requires fundamentally different capabilities
- Talent retention as company becomes more institutional
