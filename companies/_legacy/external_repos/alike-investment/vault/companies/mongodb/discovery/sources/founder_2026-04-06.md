# MongoDB — CEO/Leadership Profile

## Dev Ittycheria (前CEO, 2014-2025.11)
- **教育**: Rutgers University电气工程学士
- **创业历史**:
  - Co-founded Applica → 与Breakaway Solutions合并
  - Co-founded BladeLogic (CEO) → $900M被BMC收购 (2008)
  - Greylock Partners合伙人 (2012-2014)
  - MongoDB CEO (2014-2025.11): $40M → ~$2B收入
- **管理哲学**:
  - 受Andy Grove "High Output Management"影响
  - "You can fool people above you, sometimes those around you, but never those below you"
  - 需要incredibly judgmental来drive excellence
  - 适应型领导："how I was as a CEO vs how I am today are two very different people"
  - 通过提问引导思考，而非命令

## CJ Desai (现任CEO, 2025.11起)
- **背景**:
  - Oracle（参与首个云服务发布）
  - Symantec, EMC — 高管角色
  - ServiceNow COO/President: 从$1.5B到$10B+ annualized revenue
  - Cloudflare产品工程President
- **25年+**云基础设施、企业软件、网络安全、AI、大规模SaaS经验
- **战略方向**:
  - 开发者→C-suite市场拓展
  - AI应用是核心机遇
  - Multicloud是竞争壁垒
  - 国际市场（印度等）是增长区域
  - "Relentlessly close to customers"

## CEO认知评估
- Ittycheria是A+级别的enterprise CEO（3次成功）
- Desai的ServiceNow经历（$1.5B→$10B）证明scale-up能力极强
- MongoDB的CEO接力是高质量的——两任CEO都是proven operator
