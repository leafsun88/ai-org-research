## Business Model

### Revenue Streams
1. **Subscription SaaS** (core): per-employee-per-module pricing across HR/IT/Finance clouds
2. **Payments/Payroll float**: interest income on payroll funds in transit
3. **Corporate Cards**: interchange revenue from Rippling corporate card
4. **PEO/EOR services**: premium for global employer-of-record services
5. **Insurance brokerage**: commissions from benefits administration

### Pricing Model
- Modular: customers choose which clouds/modules to deploy
- Per-employee-per-month (PEPM) pricing
- Starts at $8/month/employee for core platform; scales up with modules
- Land-and-expand: avg customer adds modules over time (200% NDR reflects this)

### Unit Economics (estimated)
- Strong cross-sell engine: each new product adds incremental PEPM revenue on existing customer base
- New product launch economics: reaches $1M ARR in 5-6 months
- High switching cost moat: deep integration across HR/IT/Finance creates stickiness
