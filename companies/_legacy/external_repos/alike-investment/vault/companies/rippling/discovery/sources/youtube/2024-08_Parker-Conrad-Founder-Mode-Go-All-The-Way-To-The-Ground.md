# Parker Conrad: Founders Should "Go All The Way To The Ground"

## Source
- **Publisher**: TechCrunch
- **Guest**: Parker Conrad (Rippling CEO)
- **Date**: August 2024
- **Context**: Parker Conrad's management philosophy

## Key Topics
1. Founder Mode vs. Manager Mode debate
2. Why Parker believes deep founder involvement beats delegation
3. How this philosophy shapes Rippling's organizational structure
4. Parallels with other "founder mode" CEOs

## Key Insights
- Conrad advocates for founders staying deeply involved in operational details
- Rejects the "hire great people and get out of the way" conventional wisdom
- Believes this approach is what enables Rippling to ship products faster than point-solution competitors
- The compound startup model requires CEO-level coordination across product lines
- This philosophy predated but aligned with the "Founder Mode" essay that went viral in 2024
