# How to Build the Future: Parker Conrad (Y Combinator)

## Source
- **Podcast**: Y Combinator Startup Podcast — How to Build the Future
- **Host**: Garry Tan (YC CEO)
- **Guest**: Parker Conrad (Rippling CEO)
- **Date**: January 2025

## Key Topics
1. Rippling's origin story and compound startup thesis
2. Lessons from previous companies (Zenefits)
3. How AI is changing enterprise software
4. The future of "compound" software startups
5. Rippling's organizational design and hiring philosophy

## Key Insights
- Conrad shared his full origin story: from founding Zenefits, to ousting, to building Rippling
- Defined the compound startup concept: broad portfolio of interoperable products on shared infrastructure
- Discussed how AI will transform enterprise software and Rippling's positioning
- Emphasized the importance of hiring former founders to lead product lines
- Described how Rippling's data layer (employee graph) enables compound advantages
- Valued at $13.5B at the time of interview (pre-Series G)

- **D1 (CEO Cognition)**: Deep CEO voice on strategy, vision, and organizational design
