# How Parker Conrad's Revenge Fantasy Got Rippling Started (Sequoia Capital)

## Source
- **Podcast**: Sequoia Capital Podcast
- **Host**: Brian Halligan (Co-Founder HubSpot, Partner Sequoia)
- **Guest**: Parker Conrad (Rippling CEO)
- **Date**: November 2025

## Key Topics
1. The Zenefits ousting and emotional aftermath
2. How "revenge fantasy" motivated founding Rippling
3. Building Rippling into a $17B company
4. The Deel corporate espionage case
5. CEO mindset and competitive drive

## Key Insights
- Conrad openly admits Rippling was born from a "revenge fantasy" after being ousted from Zenefits
- The revenge motivation "kept me going for the first couple years" but evolved into genuine mission
- Discussed the Deel espionage case: reporters sent Rippling internal Slack messages that appeared to come from Deel
- Conrad described how the investigation unfolded and why he was certain the leak came from Deel
- Built Rippling to $17B by rejecting the conventional wisdom that startups should focus on one product
