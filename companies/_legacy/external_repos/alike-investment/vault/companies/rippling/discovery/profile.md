## Business Description

Rippling is a unified employee management platform that connects HR, IT, and Finance systems on a single data layer. Parker Conrad coined the term "compound startup" to describe Rippling's strategy: instead of building a single best-of-breed product, Rippling builds a broad portfolio of interoperable products on shared infrastructure (employee graph, workflow engine, permissions, reporting).

Core thesis: employee data is the connective tissue across all workforce systems. By owning that data layer, Rippling can ship new product modules faster than point-solution competitors and cross-sell at scale ($5M+ net new ARR monthly from cross-sell alone).

## Parker Conrad — CEO Profile

- **Background**: Harvard (The Harvard Crimson writer), previously co-founded Zenefits (2013), grew it to ~$4.5B valuation before being ousted in 2016 over compliance issues
- **Management philosophy**: "Founder Mode" — believes founders should "go all the way to the ground" to run their companies; rejects delegation-heavy management
- **Product vision**: Coined "compound startup" concept; believes conventional wisdom about focus is wrong for certain types of software businesses
- **Ownership**: ~14% of Rippling (per Bloomberg, ~$2.3B stake at $16.8B valuation)
- **Personality**: Competitive, chip-on-shoulder from Zenefits experience; describes Rippling as born from a "revenge fantasy"
- **Hiring 50+ former founders**: Runs product org by recruiting ex-founders to own product lines, parallelizing execution

## Deel Espionage Lawsuit (Key Event)
- March 2025: Rippling sued Deel for corporate espionage, alleging Deel planted a spy in Rippling's Dublin office
- Allegations: spy conducted 6,000+ Slack searches, leaked sales pipeline data, pricing, and customer data to Deel
- Deel CEO Alex Bouaziz personally implicated in amended complaint (June 2025)
- Banking records showed Deel-linked payments to the confessed spy
- Federal judge allowed case to proceed under RICO and trade secret statutes
- DOJ opened investigation into the allegations
- Deel counter-sued, alleging Rippling violated Russia sanctions
