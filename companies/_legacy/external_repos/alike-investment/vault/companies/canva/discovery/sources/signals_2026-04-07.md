### HIGH: Chief Algorithms Officer Creation
- Nirmal Govind (ex-Netflix VP Data Science, 8 years) appointed 2026

### MEDIUM: Acquisition Acceleration
- 2024: Affinity + Leonardo.AI (2 major deals)

### MEDIUM: Former CFO Departure
