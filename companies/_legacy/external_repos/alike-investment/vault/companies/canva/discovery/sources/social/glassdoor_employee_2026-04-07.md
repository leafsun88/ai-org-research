## Positive Themes
- Supportive culture, great colleagues
- Strong emphasis on impact

## Negative Themes (2025-2026)

- After-hours social events as norm, blurred work-home boundary
- Layoffs occurred despite prior promises of "never having layoffs"
- Lack of transparent communication during layoffs

- Limited promotion opportunities since external hiring wave
- "Recognition is minimal, raises are predetermined, promotions are murky"

## Organizational Signal

- Layoff communication gap suggests possible organizational growing pains
- 41% women in workforce (vs 28% tech industry avg) -- strong diversity
