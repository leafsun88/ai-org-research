# Canva -- Company Profile (Discovery)

## Overview
- **Founded**: 2013, Sydney, Australia
- **Founders**: Melanie Perkins (CEO), Cliff Obrecht (COO), Cameron Adams (CPO)
- **Status**: Private (IPO expected H2 2026)
- **Sector**: Design Software / Creative Tools / SaaS
- **HQ**: Sydney, Australia

## Business Model
- Freemium SaaS: Free tier + Canva Pro ($120/yr) + Canva Teams + Canva Enterprise
- 265M monthly active users, 31M paid seats
- $4B ARR (2025), growing 35-40% YoY
- 7 consecutive years of profitability

## Products
- **Canva Editor**: Core design platform (presentations, social, video, docs)
- **Magic Studio**: AI command center (24B+ uses)
- **Canva Code**: AI coding tool
- **Canva Sheets**: Spreadsheet product
- **Visual Suite 2.0**: Productivity + creativity platform
- **Affinity**: Professional design suite (vector, photo, layout) -- made free
- **Leonardo.AI**: Generative AI (Creative Engine API)

## Valuation & Funding
- **Current**: $42B (Aug 2025 employee share sale)

- Key investors: Blackbird Ventures, Sequoia Capital, Fidelity, JPMorgan AM, Mary Meeker/Bond Capital
