# Canva Founder/CEO Research: Melanie Perkins + Cliff Obrecht

## Melanie Perkins (CEO & Co-Founder)
- Age: ~38 (born 1987, Perth, Australia)
- Education: University of Western Australia (Communications)
- Forbes net worth: US$7.6B (Feb 2026)
- Co-founded Fusion Books (yearbook design) at 19, pivoted to Canva

### Leadership Philosophy
- "The best leaders make themselves unnecessary" -- autonomy & shared responsibility
- "Column B" thinking -- envision improbable future, work backward
- Core values: "Be a good human", "Empower others", "Make complex things simple"
- Ethical AI advocate: augment creativity, not automate
- Hands-on at events like Canva Create
- Gender-neutral hiring: 41% women (vs 28% tech avg)
- Psychological safety champion

### Management Style
- Inclusive, visionary, empathy-driven
- Collaborative environment, employees encouraged to share ideas and take risks
- Relentless user-experience focus
- Transparency, ownership, inclusivity as pillars

### Philanthropy (Two-Step Plan)
- Step 1: Build valuable company
- Step 2: Use it for good
- Pledged 80%+ equity to Canva Foundation
- $2.5B product value donated to nonprofits/educators
- $150M+ cash grants (GiveDirectly partnership)
- Focus: education, poverty alleviation, nonprofit support

### Key Public Appearances (2025-2026)
- Fortune interview (Apr 2025): Canva Code + Sheets launch
- Yahoo Finance: "$42B company" profile
- TIME Next Generation Leaders
- VivaTech keynote
- Canva Create 2025 (Visual Suite 2.0)
- Canva Create 2026 (Apr 16, SoFi Stadium)

## Cliff Obrecht (COO & Co-Founder)
- Co-founded Canva with Melanie Perkins (also life partner)
- Owns 18% of company
- Focus: operations, strategy, business growth storytelling

### Key Insights (2025-2026)
- 20VC Podcast: "Journey from 100 VC rejections to $40BN company"
- SaaStr: "$0 to $4B ARR" lessons
- Revenue 2025: "very close if not at $4B", growing ~40%, reaccelerating
- AI philosophy: "AI amplifies existing strengths, doesn't create new ones"
- ~10% of revenue to AI infrastructure/model providers
- Paused feature development to restructure platform when tech debt threatened
- Values: transparency, long-term growth over short-term wins

### Key Quotes
- "This round has been significantly oversubscribed" (Aug 2025 secondary)
- "Good enough is not good enough" (on product quality)
- On hiring: "The secret to hiring non-obvious talent"
- On wealth: "Relationships to money and why they are giving away billions"
