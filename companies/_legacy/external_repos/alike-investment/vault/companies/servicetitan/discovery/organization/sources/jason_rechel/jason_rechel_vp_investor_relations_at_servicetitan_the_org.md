Jason Rechel, CFA, currently serves as VP of Investor Relations at ServiceTitan since September 2024. Previously, Jason held key roles in investor relations and equity research, including Head of Investor Relations at Sprout Social, Inc. and various positions at Columbia Wanger Asset Management, Needham & Company, and Oppenheimer & Co. Inc., specializing in semiconductor and technology sector analysis. Notably, Jason received recognition as the #1 IR professional in the 2023 Mid Cap Software Rankings. Jason's educational background includes a BA in Economics from Bucknell University and a Finance degree from City, University of London.
