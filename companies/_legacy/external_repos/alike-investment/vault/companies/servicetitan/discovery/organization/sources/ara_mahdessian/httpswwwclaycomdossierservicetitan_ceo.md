Who is the CEO of ServiceTitan?

Ara Mahdessian, an Armenian immigrant from Iran, co-founded ServiceTitan with Vahe Kuzoyan after observing the challenges their parents faced while manually managing their home and commercial services companies. Mahdessian, who studied software engineering at Stanford University, envisioned ServiceTitan as a transformative force in the contracting industry, aiming to automate and streamline operations to improve efficiency and profitability for contractors. Under his leadership, the company has grown to serve over 11,800 trade customers and raised over $1.4 billion from notable investors, reaching a valuation of $7.3 billion. Mahdessian's visionary and empathetic approach, with a strong focus on innovation, customer success, and community building within the trades, has significantly influenced the industry's adoption of technology for operational efficiency and growth.

Ara Mahdessian co-founded ServiceTitan and has been serving as its CEO since the company's inception. As the co-founder, he has been with ServiceTitan since it was established on January 1, 2013. This means that Ara Mahdessian has been part of ServiceTitan for over 9 years, playing a crucial role in its growth and success in the home and commercial services industry.
