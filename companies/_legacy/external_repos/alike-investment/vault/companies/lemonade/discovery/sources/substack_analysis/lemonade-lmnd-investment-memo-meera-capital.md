# Lemonade (LMND) Investment Memo

## Investment Thesis

No industry is safe from innovation. Lemonade represents a disruptive force in insurance, appealing strongly to younger demographics through contemporary branding and digital-first operations.

## Company Overview

- Sector: Financial Services
- Industry: Insurance
- Offerings: Renters, pet, and homeowners insurance
- Competitors: State Farm, Berkshire Hathaway, Farmers Insurance, Liberty Mutual

## Business Strategy: Industry Rebranding

The company transforms insurance perception through:
- Entirely digital operations via mobile app and web platform
- AI-powered policy creation and claim processing
- Charitable component: 20% of unclaimed premiums directed to customer-selected charities
- Fixed-price transparency model eliminating traditional quote variability

## Technology & Operations

- AI systems handle approximately one-third of claims within 3-4 seconds
- Chatbots gathered over 100x more data points compared to traditional competitors
- 26% of tickets handled by AI as of late 2019

## Market Dynamics

- Global property, casualty, and life insurance: $5 Trillion annually
- Primary demographic: 70% under age 35; 90% represent new customer acquisition
- Customers show 56% spending increases after three years

## Competitive Moat: Conscious Capitalism

- Strong ESG alignment appeals to younger investors
- 54% of Millennial retail investors prefer ESG positive companies
- Brand perception shifts insurance from necessary evil to values-aligned choice

## Financial Metrics

- Loss ratio improved from 166% to 73% by Q4 2019
- Industry benchmark: 71.4% (2018)
- Land and expand customer acquisition strategy

## Growth Opportunities

- Geographic expansion: 41 U.S. states currently; European licensing across 31 countries
- Product line expansion beyond current offerings
- Lifecycle-triggered insurance needs among young customer base

## Risk Factors

- No recorded earnings or positive free cash flow (as of 2020)
- Charitable donation commitments extend profitability timeline
- Company age: approximately 4 years (as of 2020)
- Established competitors launching digital-first alternatives
