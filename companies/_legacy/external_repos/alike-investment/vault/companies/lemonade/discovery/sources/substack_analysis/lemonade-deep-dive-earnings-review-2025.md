# Lemonade Deep Dive and Earnings Review 2025

The insurance company is beating top line estimates and guiding higher.

Lemonade Inc. (NYSE: LMND) reported its Q1 2025 earnings, showcasing a revenue of $151.2 million, marking a 27% increase year-over-year and surpassing analyst expectations of $145.92 million. Despite this growth, the company reported a net loss of $62.4 million, translating to an EPS of -$0.86, which was better than the anticipated loss of -$0.92 per share. This performance indicates the company's continued expansion and its efforts to manage losses effectively.
