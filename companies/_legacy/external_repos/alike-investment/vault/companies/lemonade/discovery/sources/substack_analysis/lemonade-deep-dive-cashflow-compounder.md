# Lemonade (Deep Dive)

An "insurance" company, redefining the industry-wide underwriting standard.

## Company Fundamentals

Lemonade is an AI-powered insurance provider offering renters, homeowners, car, pet health, and term life insurance to over 2.9 million customers. The company emphasizes improved customer satisfaction, significantly lower costs, and more efficient underwriting through its technology-driven approach.

## Business Model Innovation

Rather than retaining all premium revenue, Lemonade allocates approximately 75% to claims and charitable giveback through its B-Corp structure, creating customer alignment. This model eliminates the traditional conflict of interest where insurers profit from denying claims.

## Financial Performance

- 2024 revenue: $527 million (60% CAGR since 2021)
- In-Force Premium exceeded $1.2 billion
- First full year of positive adjusted FCF achieved in 2024
- Gross Loss Ratio improved to 62% by Q3 2025 (industry-leading)
- Net margin expansion from -116% (2022) to -26% (TTM)

## Strategic Priorities

Management targets sustained 20%+ annual growth, loss ratios below 70%, positive adjusted EBITDA in 2026, and profitability by 2027.

## Competitive Advantages

Proprietary AI models, billion-point telematics datasets, operating leverage, and bundling strategy create a developing moat against traditional carriers and other insurtech competitors.

## Valuation Assessment

Management's profitability targets appear achievable, with DCF modeling suggesting IRR over 10% assuming reasonable future FCF multiples.

## Risk Factors

Primary concerns include exposure from reduced reinsurance dependency, potential AI model scaling failures in complex insurance lines, and acquisition cost pressures from competitive intensity.
