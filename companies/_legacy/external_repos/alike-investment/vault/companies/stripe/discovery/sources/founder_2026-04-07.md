# Founder/CEO Search: Patrick & John Collison

## Patrick Collison (CEO & Co-founder)

### Recent Public Appearances
1. **Stripe Sessions 2025 AMA** — Unscripted Q&A with Claire Hughes Johnson. Topics: stablecoin adoption barriers, local payment methods, SEPA settlement, AI applications, and what they'd do differently building Stripe again.
2. **Retool Summit** — 45-minute fireside chat with David Hsu on AI's impact on software and product development.
3. **Dwarkesh Podcast** — "Craft, Beauty, & The Future of Payments" -- deep dive on programming languages, AI, and Stripe's future.
4. **CNBC Disruptor 50 (2025)** — Stripe recognized as top disruptor.

### Strategic Views
- Two tailwinds shaping next decade of commerce: **AI** and **stablecoins**
- On stablecoins (2026 CNBC): "Stablecoin, we talked about this, but they're finally starting to work for real use cases, and we are seeing very real Stablecoin volumes."
- Culture philosophy: "You can't delegate culture" (from Elad Gil interview)
- Startup principles: speed of execution, hiring quality, focus on developers

### Leadership Style
- Known for deep technical engagement
- Maintains direct involvement in product decisions
- Emphasizes craft and beauty in payments infrastructure
- Long-term thinker -- comfortable staying private despite $159B valuation

## John Collison (President & Co-founder)

### Recent Public Appearances
1. **CNBC Interview (Feb 24, 2026)** — Discussed new tender offer, software sell-off, and impact of AI
2. **CNBC Interview (Feb 27, 2025)** — Road to profitability, utility of platform, growth strategy
3. **Irish Times Interview (May 2025)** — "I am baffled by companies doing an about-face on social initiatives"
4. **CoinDesk (May 2025)** — Stripe exploring bank partnerships on stablecoins

### Key Strategic Statements
- On enterprise growth: Enterprise giants like Microsoft and Nvidia increasingly turning to Stripe, along with "really fast growing cohort" of AI companies
- On AI: "AI is really acting as a tailwind for the business"
- On stablecoins: "A lot of our future payment volume is going to be in stablecoins" -- pointing to costly FX fees and multi-day processing as pain points
- On growth: TPV increased to $1.9T in 2025, up 34% YoY. Revenue suite on track for $1B ARR in 2026
- On IPO: "For us right now, an IPO would be a solution in search of a problem" -- business is at an "incredible moment of takeoff" and IPO would distract

### Social Values
- Baffled by companies retreating from social initiatives
- Stripe continues climate and social programs (e.g., Young Scientist Technology Exhibition sponsorship)
