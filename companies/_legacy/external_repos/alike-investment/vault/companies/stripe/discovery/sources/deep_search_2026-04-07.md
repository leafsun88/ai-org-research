## 4. Controversy & Risk

### FTC Debanking Warning (Mar 2026)
- FTC issued warning against Stripe and 3 other payment processors for "debanking of law-abiding citizens over political, religious or certain law-abiding activities"
- Significant regulatory attention

### Regulatory Tightening
- Stricter KYC/AML and security controls required for accounts in 2026
- UK FCA and Central Bank of Ireland requiring updated verification for Know Your Customer (KYC) and ultimate beneficial owners (UBO)
- Netherlands: stricter business type requirements for NL accounts (KvK compliance)

### Firearms Policy Shift
- Moved firearms businesses from "prohibited" to "restricted" category
- Now allowing with additional compliance paperwork

### Fraud Scale
- Stripe's fraud system blocked $2.3B in fraudulent activity in 2025
- AI-powered risk prevention increasingly central

### Account Policy Enforcement
- Explicitly prohibits account sales, transfers, sharing -- addressing growing black market for verified Stripe accounts
