# AI: Diving into OpenAI vs Anthropic Financial Growth Metrics

## Context

Examines the financial operating models and capital expenditure strategies of OpenAI and Anthropic, two leading LLM AI companies preparing for major IPOs.

## Revenue Growth Comparison

Both companies employ similar annualized revenue (ARR) methodologies:
- OpenAI multiplies recent four-week revenue by 13
- Anthropic combines API revenue (x13) with monthly subscription revenue (x12)

Key figures:
- OpenAI: $25 billion annualized revenue (nearly 4x YoY)
- Anthropic: $19 billion annualized revenue (approximately 14x YoY)
- Monthly revenue gap: OpenAI generates $1.7 billion versus Anthropic's $750 million

## Revenue Recognition Differences

- OpenAI: Counts 20% of Microsoft Azure sales as direct revenue, treating Microsoft as the "principal"
- Anthropic: Reports cloud provider sales as gross revenue before accounting for distributor payouts as sales/marketing expenses

## Gross Margin Trends

- Anthropic: Recently lowered gross margin projections to 40% for 2025, down 10 points from earlier estimates. Inference costs exceeded projections by 23%.
- OpenAI: Projects 46% gross margins in 2025. ~95% of ChatGPT's 900 million weekly active users don't pay.

## Capital Expenditure and Training Costs

- Anthropic: $4.1 billion in 2025 model training costs. Committed to purchasing $21 billion in Google TPUs.
- OpenAI: Spent $9.4 billion on compute for model training. Developing proprietary inference chips.

## Cash Flow Projections

- Anthropic: Anticipates turning cash flow positive by 2028 (two years earlier than OpenAI)
- Despite rapid revenue growth, both face substantial cash burn:
  - Anthropic: $5.2 billion EBITDA losses
  - OpenAI: $21.2 billion pre-tax losses

## Enterprise Customer Base

- Anthropic: ~86% of revenue from API sales to businesses, at least nine customers spending over $100 million annually. Microsoft's spending alone tracks toward $500 million.
- OpenAI: ~40% from enterprise, implying ~$5.2 billion in business revenue vs Anthropic's $3.9 billion.

## IPO Preparation

Investment bankers anticipate Anthropic's IPO preceding OpenAI's, believing public markets favor its enterprise focus and faster profitability timeline.
