# Anthropic Raising $5B at $170B Valuation

## Fundraising Details

Anthropic, the AI startup behind Claude, is reportedly raising $3-5 billion at a staggering $170 billion valuation, nearly tripling its value from March 2025.

Key details:
- Valuation increased substantially from earlier $2.8 billion seed-stage valuations
- Iconiq Capital leads the round
- Potential co-investors include Qatar Investment Authority and Singapore's GIC

This represents significant momentum for the company, reflecting strong investor confidence in its AI capabilities and market position relative to competitors.
