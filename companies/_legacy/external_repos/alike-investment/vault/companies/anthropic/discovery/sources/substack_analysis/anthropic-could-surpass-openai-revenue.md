# Anthropic Could Surpass OpenAI in Annualized Revenue by Mid-2026

## Key Finding

Since each company hit $1B in annualized revenues, Anthropic has grown substantially faster (10x vs 3.4x per year) and could overtake OpenAI by mid-2026 if recent trends continue.

## Growth Rate Comparison

- Anthropic: 10x per year since hitting $1B ARR
- OpenAI: 3.4x per year since hitting $1B ARR

## Caveats

Extrapolating recent trends is an aggressive way to forecast revenues. Growth rates this high often slow as companies scale. Both companies expect slower growth in 2026 than these trend lines imply. Still, a crossover is likely to happen this year or next.

Since July 2025, Anthropic has grown its revenue at a rate of 7x/year rather than 10x, suggesting some deceleration.

## Data Source

Analysis from Epoch AI's database which collects reports on revenues, valuations, staff counts, and more across AI companies.
