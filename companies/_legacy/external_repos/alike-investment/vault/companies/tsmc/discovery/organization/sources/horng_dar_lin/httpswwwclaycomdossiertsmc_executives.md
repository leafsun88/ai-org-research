The leadership team at TSMC is the driving force behind the company's strategic direction and key decision-making processes. They ensure that every move aligns with TSMC's mission, fostering a culture of innovation and growth that keeps the company at the forefront of the semiconductor industry.

Bringing together expertise from various fields, this team excels in operational excellence and collaboration. Their diverse backgrounds and unified vision enable TSMC to navigate complex challenges and seize new opportunities, propelling the company to new heights.

1) Rick Cassidy

Rick Cassidy, the Senior Vice President of Corporate Strategy Development at TSMC, plays a crucial role in shaping the company's long-term strategic initiatives. His responsibilities include overseeing the development and implementation of strategies that drive TSMC's growth and competitive edge in the semiconductor industry.

Throughout his tenure at TSMC, Cassidy has made significant contributions, including leading the establishment of TSMC Arizona. His leadership has been instrumental in expanding TSMC's global footprint and enhancing its operational capabilities.

With a background that includes a degree from the United States Military Academy at West Point, Cassidy brings a wealth of experience and strategic acumen to TSMC. His previous roles within the company, such as President and CEO, have equipped him with a deep understanding of the industry and the skills necessary to drive innovation and success.

2) Wendell Huang

Wendell Huang, as the Senior Vice President, Finance and Chief Financial Officer at TSMC, is responsible for overseeing the company's financial operations and strategies. His main areas of expertise include financial planning, risk management, and investor relations.

Huang's professional journey is marked by a strong background in finance, with key skills in strategic financial management and corporate governance. He has led several critical projects that have strengthened TSMC's financial stability and growth.

Through his leadership, Huang has significantly impacted TSMC by enhancing its financial performance and ensuring robust fiscal health. His strategic insights and financial acumen continue to drive the company's success in the competitive semiconductor industry.

3) Chris Horng-Dar Lin

Chris Horng-Dar Lin, the Vice President, Corporate Information Technology, and Chief Information Officer at TSMC, is responsible for overseeing the company's IT strategy and infrastructure. His contributions include enhancing TSMC's digital capabilities and ensuring robust cybersecurity measures.

Lin's career spans several high-profile roles, including VP Information Technology at Mozilla and Director of Enterprise Infrastructure Platform at Facebook. His extensive experience in IT and engineering has equipped him with specialized skills crucial for driving TSMC's technological advancements.

At TSMC, Lin manages a team of IT professionals and leads initiatives focused on digital transformation and operational efficiency. His leadership ensures that TSMC remains at the cutting edge of technology in the semiconductor industry.

4) David Keller

David Keller, the President and Chief Executive Officer of TSMC North America, is responsible for driving the company's strategic initiatives and overseeing its operations in the region. His areas of expertise include business management, strategic planning, and operational leadership.

With a career spanning over four decades, Keller has held various leadership roles at TSMC and other prominent semiconductor companies. Notable successes include his tenure as President and CEO of TSMC North America, where he has led significant projects and teams, contributing to the company's growth and market presence.

5) Paul de Bot

Paul de Bot, the President of TSMC Europe, is responsible for overseeing the company's operations and strategic initiatives across the EMEA region. His leadership has been pivotal in driving TSMC's growth and market presence in Europe, ensuring the company's continued success in the semiconductor industry.

With a career spanning over three decades, de Bot brings extensive experience in business development, strategy, and marketing. His notable achievements include founding Merapar and holding key roles at NXP Semiconductors and Philips Digital Networks, where he led significant projects that enhanced operational efficiency and market expansion.

6) Makoto Onodera

Makoto Onodera, the President of TSMC Japan, is responsible for overseeing the company's operations and strategic initiatives in the region. His expertise lies in business management and operational leadership, ensuring TSMC Japan's continued growth and success in the semiconductor industry.

Onodera's career includes significant roles such as Supervisor of Dielectric CVD at Applied Materials Japan and President of TSMC Japan since 1997. He holds an MBA from Willamette University, which has equipped him with strong strategic and managerial skills, contributing to his effective leadership and successful project management.

7) Lora Ho

Lora Ho, serving as the Senior Vice President, Human Resources at TSMC, is responsible for overseeing the company's HR strategies and initiatives. Her main areas of expertise include talent management, organizational development, and employee relations.
