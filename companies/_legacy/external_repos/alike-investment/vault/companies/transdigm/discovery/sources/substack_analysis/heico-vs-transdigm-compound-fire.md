# Heico vs. TransDigm: A Battle in the Aerospace Arena

Comparative analysis evaluating two aerospace component manufacturers — Heico Corporation (HEI) and TransDigm Group (TDG) — using the author's proprietary "Quick Scan" investment assessment framework.

## Key Findings

### Heico Corporation (Winner)
- Investment Readiness Score: 83.1% (exceeds 80% threshold)
- Net Debt/EBITDA: 2.2x
- Operating Cash Flow/Net Income: 143%
- 10-year revenue CAGR: 14%
- Gross margin: 39%
- Insider ownership: 14%
- Stock-based compensation: 1%

### TransDigm Group
- Investment Readiness Score: 74.3% (below threshold)
- Net Debt/EBITDA: 4.8x
- Operating Cash Flow/Net Income: 122.2%
- 10-year revenue CAGR: 12.7%
- Gross margin: 59%
- Insider ownership: 0.4%
- Stock-based compensation: 2.7%

## Decision Rationale

Heico emerges victorious despite TransDigm's superior ROIC (15.6% vs. 12%) and higher gross margins. The determining factors include Heico's stronger balance sheet stability, superior cash conversion efficiency, significantly higher management ownership alignment, and lower shareholder dilution through compensation. While TransDigm achieved slightly better stock performance historically, Heico's lower-risk profile better aligns with quality investing principles emphasizing risk mitigation.
