Who is the CFO of Synopsys?

Shelagh Glaser, the current Chief Financial Officer (CFO) at Synopsys, has an impressive professional background spanning across the software and semiconductor industries. Prior to joining Synopsys, Glaser held the position of CFO at Zendesk, a software-as-a-service CRM company, where she led global finance and various other responsibilities. 

She also spent 29 years at Intel, serving as a corporate vice president and taking on the roles of CFO and Chief Operating Officer for the Data Platform Group and the Client Computing Group. In addition to her executive roles, she serves on the board of PubMatic, a digital advertising supply chain leader, as a member of the audit committee.

Education History

Shelagh Glaser holds a diverse educational background, with degrees from prestigious institutions. She earned her Master of Business Administration (MBA) in Finance from Carnegie Mellon University, a top-ranked business school. Glaser also completed an Executive Education program at Stanford University, specifically the Stanford Executive Accelerator for Intel, which provided her with valuable leadership and management skills. Additionally, she holds a Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) in Economics from the University of Michigan, further solidifying her strong foundation in finance and economics.

When did Shelagh Glaser join Synopsys?

Shelagh Glaser joined Synopsys as the Chief Financial Officer (CFO) on December 1, 2022. Since then, she has been overseeing the Finance department and other business operations within the company. As of now, Glaser has been with Synopsys for a few months, bringing her extensive financial and operational experience from her previous roles at Zendesk and Intel to contribute to the company's growth and industry leadership.
