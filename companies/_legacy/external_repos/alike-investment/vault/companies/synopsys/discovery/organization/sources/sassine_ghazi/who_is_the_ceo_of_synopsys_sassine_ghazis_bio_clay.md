Who is the CEO of Synopsys?

Sassine Ghazi, a seasoned leader with a 25-year history at Synopsys, has held various positions within the company, including roles in applications engineering, product development, sales, and operations. 

Before joining Synopsys in 1998, Ghazi worked as a design engineer at Intel. He holds a bachelor's degree in business administration from the Lebanese American University, a B.S.E.E. from the Georgia Institute of Technology, and an M.S.E.E. from the University of Tennessee. 

As CEO, Ghazi is known for his positive attitude, strong bias for action, and ability to attract and promote outstanding talent. Under his leadership, Synopsys has launched several groundbreaking solutions and experienced record revenue growth and profitability.

Education History

Sassine Ghazi holds a diverse educational background, with degrees from three prestigious institutions. He earned a Bachelor's Degree in Electrical Engineering from the Georgia Institute of Technology, a Master of Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, and a Bachelor's Degree in Business from the Lebanese American University. This combination of technical and business education has provided Ghazi with a solid foundation for his successful career in the technology industry.

When did Sassine Ghazi join Synopsys?

Sassine Ghazi joined Synopsys in 1998 and has since held various positions within the company, contributing to its growth and success. He became the CEO on January 1, 2024, after serving in roles such as Chief Operating Officer and President. With a 25-year tenure at Synopsys, Ghazi has played a significant role in driving innovation and growth, launching groundbreaking solutions such as the Fusion Design Platform™ and Synopsys.ai.
