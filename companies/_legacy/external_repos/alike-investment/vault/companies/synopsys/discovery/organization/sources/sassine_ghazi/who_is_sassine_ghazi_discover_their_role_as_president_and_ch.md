Sassine Ghazi is the President and Chief Executive Officer of Synopsys, a global leader in electronic design automation (EDA) and semiconductor IP. With a career at Synopsys spanning over two decades, Ghazi has risen through the ranks, starting as an applications engineer in 1998. His deep technical expertise and strong business acumen have been instrumental in the company's growth. Before becoming CEO in January 2024, he served as Chief Operating Officer, where he was responsible for driving strategy and execution across all business units. Ghazi is a key architect of Synopsys' strategy in AI-driven chip design and has been pivotal in expanding the company's focus from silicon to complex systems, addressing the growing convergence of hardware and software.

What Are Sassine Ghazi's Key Achievements?

Appointment as President and CEO of Synopsys

In January 2024, Ghazi succeeded co-founder Aart de Geus as President and CEO, taking the helm of the company to lead its next phase of innovation and growth in the era of 'SysMoore'—the systemic complexity of advanced electronics.

Spearheaded Growth as Chief Operating Officer

As COO, he led Synopsys' core business segments, including EDA and IP, achieving significant revenue growth and strengthening the company's market leadership position across all product groups and global regions.

Championed AI in Chip Design

Ghazi was a driving force behind the development and market adoption of Synopsys.ai™, the industry's first full-stack AI-driven EDA suite, which leverages artificial intelligence to dramatically accelerate the chip design process and optimize performance.

Led Expansion into Software Integrity

He played a crucial role in expanding Synopsys' portfolio and market presence in the software integrity and security space, recognizing the critical need for secure and high-quality software in modern electronic systems.

Synopsys, Inc. is a global leader in electronic design automation (EDA) and semiconductor intellectual property (IP). The company provides a comprehensive portfolio of tools, IP, and services used by engineers to design and verify complex integrated circuits (chips) and electronic systems. Synopsys also offers software quality and security testing solutions, enabling developers to build secure, high-quality software. Its products are fundamental to the development of advanced electronics found in a wide range of applications, including mobile devices, artificial intelligence, automotive, and cloud computing.
