## 5. AI Products & Innovation (2025-2026)

### September 2025 Launch: Next-Gen AI Capabilities
- **Sprinklr Copilot**: Always-on AI companion for customer-facing teams, real-time conversational assistance across all product suites, interactive dashboard conversations, proactive system monitoring
- **Sprinklr AI Agents**: Autonomous AI agents for decision-making and task automation, built natively into the platform (not bolted on), understand platform data models, journey vocabulary, and embedded AI
- **Customer Feedback Management**: AI-native, no-code approach to modernize feedback management

### Spring 2026 (26.4) Release
- **Autonomous Evaluation**: Explainable, test-backed validation of AI agent behavior — key for enterprise trust
- **Customer Feedback Copilot**: Raw feedback → visual trends, comparisons, multi-level drilldowns via conversational prompts
- **Marketing Copilot**: Extends conversational automation to social, paid, VoC and service workflows
- **Agent Copilot**: More proactive assistance for contact center agents
- **Platform Governance**: Enhanced controls for enterprise AI deployment

### AI Strategy Assessment
- Positioning: "AI-native" platform (built from ground up, not acquired/bolted on)
- Advantage: Single codebase means AI can leverage unified data across all CX functions
- Challenge: Competing against both incumbents (Salesforce, Adobe) and AI-native startups
- Revenue impact: Unclear if AI features are driving material upsell or new customer acquisition yet
