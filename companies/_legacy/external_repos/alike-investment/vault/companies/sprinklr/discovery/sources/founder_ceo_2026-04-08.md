# Sprinklr (CXM) — Founder/CEO Research

## Ragy Thomas — Founder & Chairman

### Background
- Founded Sprinklr in September 2009
- Served as CEO from founding until June 2024
- June 2024: Appointed Co-CEO alongside Trac Pham
- November 2024: Transitioned to Founder & Chairman of the Board
- Currently also Chairman, Co-CEO and Co-Founder of UnifyApps (new venture)

### Unified CXM Vision (Core Strategic Philosophy)
- Created the "Unified-CXM" category: managing all customer operations and experiences holistically
- Strategic framework: (1) Align executives from brand-centric to customer-centric thinking, (2) Align teams to work across silos, (3) Develop processes, (4) Implement technology
- Differentiation from competitors: While Salesforce and Adobe expanded through acquisitions creating "Franken-stacks," Thomas insisted on building every line of code on a single platform
- Core belief: Data must flow seamlessly between marketing, social media, and customer service teams
- On customer loyalty: Put humans at the center of marketing

### AI Strategy
- Positioned Sprinklr as "AI-native" platform
- Spoke about AI's role in CXM at CX-Wise events
- Led development of unified platform architecture that enables AI capabilities across all CX functions
- Built AI-native platform vs competitors who bolt on AI to acquired products

### Recent Activity (per LinkedIn)
- Participated in private dinner in Bengaluru with GCC leaders on building AI-native enterprises
- Transitioning focus to UnifyApps while maintaining Chairman role at Sprinklr

## Rory Read — President & CEO (since November 2024)

### Background
- Appointed President, CEO & Board Director on November 5, 2024
- Succeeded Ragy Thomas (founder) and Trac Pham (co-CEO who moved to advisory)
- Previously served as CEO of AMD (2011-2014) and held senior roles at Lenovo and IBM

### Leadership Transition Context
- June 2024: Trac Pham named co-CEO alongside Thomas
- Revenue forecasts for FY2027 revised downward during this period
- September 2024 earnings call: Pham acknowledged "near-term challenges" including "lower net bookings" and execution challenges
- November 2024: Rory Read brought in as professional CEO to stabilize and execute turnaround

### Turnaround Mandate
- Stock down ~33% over trailing year as of March 2026
- Read tasked with operational discipline and growth re-acceleration
- $200M stock buyback authorized (March 2026) — $125M via Accelerated Share Repurchase
- FY2026 results showed improvement: Revenue $857.2M (+8% YoY), non-GAAP operating margin 17%
