# Dustin Moskovitz — Founder/CEO Research
**采集时间**: 2026-04-08

## Background
- Facebook co-founder (youngest self-made billionaire at time of founding)
- Co-founded Asana in 2008 with Justin Rosenstein (former Google/Facebook engineer)
- Stepped down as CEO in 2025 after 15+ years, transitioning to Chairman
- Also co-founded Good Ventures and Open Philanthropy (philanthropy)

## CEO Transition (March 2025)
- Announced intention to step down as CEO and transition to Chair role
- Dan Rogers named as new CEO
- Anne Raimondi as COO and head of business
- Sonalee Parekh as CFO (later replaced by Megji Aziz)
- As Chair, focusing on: product vision, strategic guidance, AI landscape navigation
- Stepping away from day-to-day operations to focus on Good Ventures and Open Philanthropy

## Leadership Philosophy

### People-First Culture
- Invested heavily in employee mental health, communication skills
- Philosophy: putting people first is the only way to get exceptional results
- Implemented policies encouraging candid team communication
- Culture built on regular check-ins, feedback loops, group brainstorming

### Self-Awareness as Leader
- Described himself as an introvert who found CEO role "quite exhausting"
- Admitted he never intended to manage people — aligned with 82% of bosses who say the same
- This self-awareness informed his decision to step down and find a better-suited operational CEO

### Mindful Business Approach
- Strong advocate for "mindful business" — integrating wellness into company culture
- Asana known for meditation rooms, emphasis on work-life balance
- Belief that companies stimulate creativity by creating environments for free expression

## Key Interview: Stratechery (October 2025)
- Ben Thompson interview covering AI, SaaS disruption, and safety
- Discussed journey from Facebook to Asana
- Addressed whether SaaS companies will be disrupted by AI
- Published post-CEO transition, reflecting on his role as Chairman

## Philanthropy & AI Safety
- Good Ventures: major philanthropic vehicle, focused on global health, criminal justice reform
- Open Philanthropy: effective altruism-aligned giving ($500M+ distributed)
- Active voice on AI safety and responsible AI development
- Transition to Chairman partly motivated by desire to dedicate more energy to global challenges

## Insider Activity (Concerning Signal)
- CFO Megji Aziz sold 30,650 shares on March 27, 2026 at $6.10/share
- COO Anne Raimondi sold ~$2.3M worth of shares in late 2025
- Pattern of insider selling during stock decline raises questions
