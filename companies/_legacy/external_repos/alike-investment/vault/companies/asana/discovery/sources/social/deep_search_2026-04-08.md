## 3. Competitive Landscape: Asana vs Monday.com vs Smartsheet

### Market Position
- Monday.com: Revenue leader, strongest growth, multi-product suite

- Asana: Smallest by revenue, weakest growth, but strong in task management UX

### Competitive Risks
- Monday.com growing 2.5x faster than Asana

## 5. AI Product Strategy

### Spring 2025 Release
- AI-powered templates and automations
- Prebuilt AI workflows in Smart Workflow Gallery
- Focus on speed and scale

### Summer 2025 Release
- AI Studio: no-code builder for custom AI workflows (launched late 2025)
- Ready-to-use AI workflows for campaigns, Kanban boards, goals
- New add-ons and role-based access controls for safe AI expansion

### Fall 2025 Release
- **AI Teammates** (beta): Assign tasks to AI agents like any team member
- AI risk reports: weekly automated risk assessments
- Multilingual semantic AI search (cross-language intent understanding)
- Comprehensive timesheets and budgeting tools

### 2026 Updates
- Smart chat: tailored answers to user role, function, use case
- Better understanding of sections and custom fields
- AI Studio: reading attachments from Dropbox and Box
- Expanded Work Graph access for better context retrieval

### Strategic Positioning
- Pivoting from "task management tool" to "AI orchestration platform"
- Leveraging Work Graph (proprietary data model) as AI differentiation
- Competition: Monday.com and Smartsheet also aggressively investing in AI
- Market skepticism: need to prove AI translates to revenue growth
