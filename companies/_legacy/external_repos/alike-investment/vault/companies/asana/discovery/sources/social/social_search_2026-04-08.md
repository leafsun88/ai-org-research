## Reddit Analysis

### User Perspectives on Asana as Product
- Teams appreciate timelines, templates, and dependencies features
- Common complaints about lack of functionality for repetitive/routine tasks
- Some users find flexibility overwhelming for smaller teams
- Frequent comparisons with ClickUp (power users), Notion (note-centric teams), Monday.com (scalable)
- Multiple Reddit threads discuss switching from Asana to alternatives like Bitrix24, ClickUp

### Competitive Positioning (Reddit Consensus)
- Asana strong for mid-size team task management
- Weakening vs Monday.com (revenue leader, 250K customers) and Smartsheet (Fortune 500 penetration)
- Linear gaining traction among software development teams as alternative
