Asana Work Graph® Hierarchy 

 The Asana Work Graph®, our data model, captures the relationships, information, and people connected to units of work—tasks, projects, portfolios, goals, and more—to enable a complete, connected, and up-to-date map of work in an organization. The Asana Work Graph provides unique capabilities, such as multi-homing, which enables tasks to be shared between multiple projects or workflows without duplication. Different teams view and share the very same tasks, reducing duplicative work and creating cross-functional clarity and accountability.
