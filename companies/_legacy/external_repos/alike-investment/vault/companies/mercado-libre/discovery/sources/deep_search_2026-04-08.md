## 4. Controversies & Risks (2025-2026)

### Margin Compression
- Operating margin fell to 11.1% (2025) from 12.7% (2024)
- Spent much of 2025 defending market share — expanded free shipping in Brazil, absorbed higher logistics costs, leaned into promotions
- Strategy successful for engagement/volume but profitability suffered

### Rising Credit Risk
- Provision for doubtful accounts rose 58% YoY (first 9 months of 2025) vs 37% revenue growth
- Credit portfolio grew 90% in Q4 2025 — aggressive expansion led to massive increase in bad debt provisions
- Key question: Is credit growth sustainable or creating systemic risk?

- **Amazon**: Expanding Latin America presence
