## Reddit & Community Discussions

### MercadoLibre's LATAM Dominance
- Largest online marketplace in Latin America with 668M monthly visits
- Almost 4x Amazon's traffic in the region
- Controls 28% of LATAM e-commerce (vs Amazon's 4%)
- Operates in 18 countries; Brazil 40% of traffic, Argentina 21%, Mexico 20%
- Surpasses Amazon controlling >55% of region's digital retail media market (vs Amazon's 17.7%)

### Ecosystem Strength
- Not just e-commerce — entire ecosystem for online and offline commerce
- Success tied to understanding LATAM consumer base + dominating payments and fulfillment
- Integrated flywheel: marketplace drives payment volume, payments generate data enabling credit, logistics underpins experience

## Substack Deep Dives

### Business Model Analysis
- 6 main segments: Libre (marketplace), Envios (logistics), Credito (credit), Shops, Mercado Pago (payments), Libre Publicidad (advertising)
- Often described as "love-child of Amazon, Square and PayPal"
- Self-reinforcing flywheel: commerce attracts users → drives payment volume → generates data/trust → enables credit → logistics locks in customers

### Fintech Scale
- Mercado Pago evolved into digital bank serving 60M+ monthly users
- Processed $197B in payments in 2024
- Total payment volume reached $14.7B in one quarter (+81.8% YoY in USD, +129.2% FX neutral)
