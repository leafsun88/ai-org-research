# Marcos Galperin — Founder/CEO Research (2025-2026)

## CEO Transition (Late 2025 → 2026)

### Timeline
- Marcos Galperin founded Mercado Libre 26 years ago (1999)
- Announced stepping down as CEO in late 2025
- Remains as Executive Chairman
- New CEO: Ariel Szarfsztejn (joined MercadoLibre in 2017)
- Transition described as swift — "in practice lasted a week" before Szarfsztejn fully took over

### Key Interviews on Transition
- **Bloomberg (Dec 2025)**: "Argentina's Richest Man, Marcos Galperin, on Stepping Down as MercadoLibre CEO" — discussed handing over while still having ideas for the business
- **Exame (Nov 2025)**: Extensive interview with both Galperin and Szarfsztejn on the transition
- **Inside MercadoLibre Podcast**: Both discussed CEO transition with Richard Cathcart
- **Feb 2025 podcast**: Galperin reflected on company's 25-year journey and DNA

## Galperin's Strategic Vision

### Trade War as Opportunity (April 2025 CNBC Interview)
- Interviewed at LatAm Tech Forum in Miami
- Argued trade war is a "very big opportunity" for Latin America
- Mexico could become the trade war's biggest beneficiary
- Discussed MercadoLibre's fintech expansion plans

### Fintech Strategy
- Launched Mercado Pago in 2003 to overcome trust and payment infrastructure deficits in LATAM
- Evolved from marketplace payment solution to cornerstone fintech service
- Keeps pushing into new sectors: mutual funds, crypto
- Diversification into fintech made Mercado Libre Latin America's second most valuable company (~$85B)

### Company Culture & DNA
- Feb 2025 podcast: Reflected on company's culture and what makes MELI unique
- Described as building not just a company but digital infrastructure for LATAM
- Philosophy of local market adaptation — tailoring services to each market's unique needs
