## 3. Competitive Positioning (vs. Schwab / Fidelity)

### Robinhood
- 23 million users
- Primarily mobile-first, self-directed investors
- Commission-free stocks, ETFs, options, crypto
- Younger demographic (Gen Z / Millennials)
- Super-app ambition: trading + banking + credit + wealth management

### Schwab
- Full-service brokerage, founded 1971
- Much broader product suite (advisory, banking, retirement)
- Tens of millions of customers
- Physical branch network

### Fidelity
- Founded 1946, longest-operating
- Largest investment selection
- Multiple platforms (web, Active Trader Pro, mobile)
- Strong retirement/401k business

### Key Differentiation
- Robinhood's edge: mobile UX, crypto integration, 24/7 trading, prediction markets, social trading
- Schwab/Fidelity edge: full-service advisory, research depth, retirement products, institutional trust
- Robinhood's super-app strategy aims to close the gap in wealth management and banking

### SEC $45M Fine (January 13, 2025)
- Violated 10+ separate securities law provisions
- Key violations:
  - Failed to accurately report trading activity
  - Failed to comply with short sale rules
  - Failed to timely file suspicious activity reports (2020-2022)

  - Failed to address known cybersecurity vulnerability (2021) — led to data breach of millions

- Customers lost money when re-entering orders
