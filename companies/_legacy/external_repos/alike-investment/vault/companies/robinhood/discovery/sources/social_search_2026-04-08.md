## Reddit / WallStreetBets Analysis

### Robinhood Social Platform vs. WallStreetBets (Sep 2025)
- Robinhood launched social trading platform directly competing with Reddit's WallStreetBets
- All posts require including a trade (equities, options, other assets) with real-time position updates
- Users can check 1-year and daily P&L and profit rate of others
- Can follow insiders, hedge funds, and politicians
- Source: Bloomberg, Yahoo Finance, InvestmentNews, Finance Magnates

### Background Context
- Before starting trading platform, founders Vlad Tenev and Baiju Bhatt originally planned an online community for sharing investing information
- Robinhood traders historically more active on social media than other brokerages' customers

### Academic Research (2024)
- Reddit posts have higher impact on retail trading than traditional news
- Positive Reddit posts lead to more purchases (trend following behavior)
- Source: ScienceDirect research paper
