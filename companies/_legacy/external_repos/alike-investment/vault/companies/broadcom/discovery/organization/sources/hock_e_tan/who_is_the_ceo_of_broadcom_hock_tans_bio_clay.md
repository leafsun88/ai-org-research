Who is the CEO of Broadcom?

Hock Tan, a Malaysian-born Chinese-American business executive, has an extensive background in the technology industry. Tan's leadership at Broadcom has been marked by significant growth through high-profile acquisitions, including semiconductor designer LSI and tech giant Brocade.

His expertise in computer-chip design and business strategy has not only contributed to Broadcom's success but also led to his recent appointment as a board member of Meta Platforms.

Education History

Hock Tan's educational background is both impressive and extensive, with degrees from prestigious institutions. He holds a Bachelor of Science (BS) and a Master of Science (MS) in Mechanical Engineering from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). In addition to his engineering degrees, Tan also earned a Master of Business Administration (MBA) from Harvard Business School, further solidifying his expertise in both technical and business fields.

When did Hock Tan join Broadcom?

Hock Tan joined Broadcom in March 2006 and has been serving as the President and Chief Executive Officer since then. As of now, he has been with the company for over 15 years, leading it through significant growth and high-profile acquisitions. Tan's extensive experience in the technology industry and his strategic leadership have contributed to Broadcom's success and expansion in the market.
