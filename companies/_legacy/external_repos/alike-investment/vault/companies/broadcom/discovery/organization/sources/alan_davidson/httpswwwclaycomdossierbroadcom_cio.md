Who is the CIO of Broadcom?

Alan Davidson, the Chief Information Officer at Broadcom, has a rich background in technology and leadership. Over the years, Davidson has held various key leadership positions at Broadcom and its legacy companies, including HP, Agilent, and Avago. His responsibilities encompass the continuous evolution of Broadcom's technology landscape, overseeing enterprise infrastructure, data center management, and cybersecurity. 

Davidson's role also includes managing e-business applications and ensuring robust data privacy measures, making him a pivotal figure in maintaining Broadcom's competitive edge in the tech industry.

Education History

Alan Davidson's educational journey began at the University of the West of Scotland, where he pursued a Bachelor of Science degree in Computing Science. His academic tenure spanned from January 1985 to January 1989, laying a solid foundation for his future career in technology and leadership. When did Alan Davidson join Broadcom?

Alan Davidson joined Broadcom in February 2016 as the Director of IT Infrastructure. He was later promoted to Chief Information Officer on December 1, 2020. With his extensive background in technology and leadership, Davidson has been instrumental in driving the company's technological advancements. As of now, he has been with Broadcom for over seven years, contributing significantly to its growth and success.
