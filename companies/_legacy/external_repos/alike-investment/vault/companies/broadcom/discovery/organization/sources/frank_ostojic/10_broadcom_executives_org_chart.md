The leadership team at Broadcom is the driving force behind the company's strategic direction. They make key decisions that align with Broadcom's mission, ensuring every move supports long-term goals and market leadership.

Bringing together expertise from various fields, this team fosters a culture of innovation and growth. Their collaborative approach drives operational excellence, making Broadcom a leader in the tech industry.

1) Alan Davidson

Alan Davidson, the Chief Information Officer at Broadcom, oversees the company's IT strategy and infrastructure. His role involves ensuring that Broadcom's technology systems align with its business goals and support its growth.

Since joining Broadcom, Davidson has spearheaded several key initiatives, including the modernization of IT infrastructure and the implementation of advanced cybersecurity measures. His efforts have significantly enhanced the company's operational efficiency and security.

Davidson's extensive background includes roles at Avago Technologies, Agilent Technologies, and Hewlett-Packard, where he honed his skills in IT architecture and program management. With a BSc in Computing Science from the University of the West of Scotland, he brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to Broadcom. For more details, visit the Broadcom CIO dossier.

2) Hock E. Tan

Hock E. Tan serves as the President, Chief Executive Officer, and Director at Broadcom, where he is responsible for the overall strategic direction and operational management of the company. His expertise in corporate leadership and financial management plays a crucial role in driving Broadcom's growth and market presence.

Tan's professional journey includes significant roles at Avago Technologies, Integrative Device Technology, and Integrated Circuit Systems, Inc., where he held various executive positions. His extensive experience in the tech industry has equipped him with key skills in strategic planning, mergers and acquisitions, and operational efficiency.

Under Tan's leadership, Broadcom has undertaken several transformative projects, enhancing its technological capabilities and market reach. His impact on the company is evident in its robust financial performance and innovative product offerings, solidifying Broadcom's position as a leader in the tech industry.

3) Mark Brazeal

Mark Brazeal, the Chief Legal and Corporate Affairs Officer at Broadcom, oversees the company's legal strategies and corporate affairs, ensuring compliance and managing legal risks. His contributions are pivotal in navigating complex legal landscapes and supporting Broadcom's strategic initiatives.

With a career spanning several high-profile roles, Brazeal has held significant positions at SanDisk Corporation and Broadcom Corporation, where he honed his expertise in legal affairs and corporate governance. His specialized skills in licensing and intellectual property have been instrumental in Broadcom's growth and innovation.

Brazeal manages a team of legal professionals and is actively involved in key industry associations, such as the Semiconductor Industry Association. His leadership extends to various initiatives that enhance Broadcom's legal framework and corporate policies, reinforcing the company's market leadership.

4) Kirsten Spears

Kirsten Spears serves as the Chief Financial Officer and Chief Accounting Officer at Broadcom, where she is responsible for overseeing the company's financial strategies, accounting practices, and regulatory compliance. Her expertise in corporate finance and accounting ensures that Broadcom maintains robust financial health and operational efficiency.

Spears' professional background includes significant roles at Avago Technologies, LSI Corporation, and PwC, where she developed her skills in corporate finance and auditing. She has successfully led various financial teams and projects, contributing to Broadcom's growth and stability.

5) Charlie Kawwas

Charlie Kawwas serves as the President of the Semiconductor Solutions Group at Broadcom, where he is responsible for driving the strategic direction and operational management of the semiconductor division. His leadership has been instrumental in advancing Broadcom's semiconductor technologies and expanding its market presence.

With a career spanning various high-profile roles, including Chief Operating Officer and Chief Sales Officer at Broadcom, Kawwas brings extensive expertise in sales, marketing, and product management. His notable achievements include leading significant projects at Avago Technologies and LSI Corporation, where he played a key role in driving sales growth and product innovation.
