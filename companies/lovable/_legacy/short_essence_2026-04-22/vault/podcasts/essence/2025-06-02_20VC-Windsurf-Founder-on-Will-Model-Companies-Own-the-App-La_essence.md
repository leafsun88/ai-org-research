# 20VC: Windsurf Founder on the App Layer

## Windsurf 视角下，AI coding 工具的 moat 是 speed 和 learning curve

Varun 对 moat 的回答是 Lovable 竞争分析里很重要的反方材料。他认为早期 AI coding startup 讲传统 moat 太早，几十个工程师做出的产品迟早会被别人复现，真正能形成优势的是速度、学习哪里会死、以及把这些教训持续 compound。这个框架同样适用于 Lovable：它的防御不在“别人不能生成 app”，而在谁更快理解非技术用户、谁能把 prototype-to-production 的坑系统化、谁能把增长和产品反馈变成复利。

“For a startup, the idea of having a moat is usually premature. You can have amazing engineers, but with 50 or 100 engineers it is still only hundreds of engineering-years in the product. Someone else can build something in the category. The only moat in our category is speed: learning where the dead bodies are and learning how to compound an advantage.” —— Varun Mohan, 20VC, 2025-06-02

## Windsurf 和 Lovable 的分叉，是 developer power tool 与 non-developer creation layer 的路线选择

Varun 对 Lovable/Bolt 的评价很直接：这些工具聚焦 non-developer，未来会被迫加入更多 tunables 和配置；Windsurf 聚焦 professional developers 和大型 codebase，长期会自然下探到非技术用户。这给 Lovable 提供了一个竞争坐标：Lovable 从 simplicity 出发，Windsurf/Cursor 从 codebase understanding 出发。双方会收敛，但短期组织能力很不同，一个要懂用户心理和默认体验，一个要懂复杂工程系统和 developer trust。

“The tools like Lovable and Bolt are very focused on the non-developer. With time, they will need to build more tunables and more ways to configure the product. For us, if we can deeply understand large codebases, it naturally follows that with very little natural language a non-technical person can build apps. But if we build for that use case right now, we give up helping developers.” —— Varun Mohan, 20VC, 2025-06-02

## Enterprise switching cost 仍然存在，但速度会不断侵蚀静态锁定

Varun 讲 enterprise business 时，承认大公司深度部署、团队协作和 wall-to-wall usage 会创造 switching cost，但他也提醒，如果产品不持续变好，再强的 switching cost 也挡不住用户迁移。这个判断对 Lovable 企业化很有用：Lovable 需要 admin/security/integration 来建立企业摩擦，但更重要的是维持 product velocity。AI coding 类工具的用户已经习惯快速切换，一旦体验落后，旧 SaaS 式锁定很难成立。

“A large enterprise business that is wall-to-wall across Fortune 500 companies has inherent switching costs. But if you focus too hard on making your product hard to switch off, and you do not improve fast enough, people will switch onto the better product despite the switching costs.” —— Varun Mohan, 20VC, 2025-06-02

## 一人十亿美元公司的怀疑，提醒 Lovable 不能把组织价值简化成“人少”

Varun 不相信 solo billion-dollar company 叙事。他的理由很朴素：如果一个人能做，两个同样聪明的人也能做，竞争会压低 margin、增加 CAC、削弱品牌垄断。这个观点可以作为 Lovable “one-person unicorn” 叙事的反方：AI 会让小团队更强，但公司价值仍来自持续投资、分发、客户关系、品牌和组织执行，不能只靠 headcount 消失。

“I never understood the idea that a single person will build a billion-dollar company. A company is the sum of discounted cash flows over time. If the AI is so good that you do not need people, everyone else will have access to the AI too, and competitive pressure increases. It is hard for one person to run away without continued investment.” —— Varun Mohan, 20VC, 2025-06-02

## First mover 的价值是组织会自我打断

Varun 对“time to clone 变短后 first mover 还有没有意义”的回答很适合 Lovable：先发代表的是一种组织能力，愿意快速打断自己、投不增量的新技术、在范式变化时 pivot。Lovable 早期从 GPT Engineer 到 Lovable 的 sequencing 也是类似逻辑：先发不保证防御，但说明团队能更早学习和纠偏。

“Being first does two things. First, it signals how you run the company. Companies that are first to a new paradigm are willing to disrupt themselves, change quickly, invest in technologies that are not incremental, and break themselves. Second, being first lets you learn faster.” —— Varun Mohan, 20VC, 2025-06-02
