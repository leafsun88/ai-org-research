# Building Lovable With Anton Osika

## Lovable 的 simplicity 是一套产品决策权：模型、速度和复杂性都由平台替用户选择

Anton 在 Building One 里把 Lovable 的 simplicity 说得很具体：用户不应该在主流程里选择快模型还是慢模型、选择哪个 AI model、怎么配置 capabilities。Lovable 把这些判断收回平台侧，通过实验决定默认体验。这个动作和 Apple 类比很像：隐藏复杂度意味着组织承担更多技术判断。Lovable 如果服务 99% 的 builder，就必须持续抵抗 power user 把界面拖复杂的冲动。

“Should the user choose a fast response or a slow response? Should they choose between different AI models and capabilities? We fundamentally think it is on our side to choose what is best for users at large. Because we simplified that much, we can run many more experiments where we switch out the AI, make it faster or slower, and see what users prefer.” —— Anton Osika, Building One, 2026-03-10

## Enterprise case 说明 Lovable 已经能替代真实预算，不只是帮团队做 prototype

Anton 提到一个大型房地产公司用 Lovable 重做 hundreds of websites：原计划一年，最后几周、两个人完成，并把原先每年 100 万美元集成成本降到约 1%。这个 case 是 Lovable 企业价值的关键证据。企业把真实对外网站和集成迁到 Lovable 上，用更少人、更短周期、更低成本完成可交付项目。这样的案例如果可复制，Lovable 的 enterprise TAM 会远大于创业者工具。

“A global real estate company had hundreds of websites in dozens of countries. The marketing leader planned a replatform that would take a year; then they found Lovable and, in a few weeks with two more people, built everything on Lovable. The previous integration cost about $1 million per year, and now they are paying roughly 1% of that underlying price.” —— Anton Osika, Building One, 2026-03-10

## 从 demo 到 business 的墙，不在生成第一版，而在测试、Polish 和让客户 care

Anton 对“用户在哪里撞墙”的回答很实际：Lovable 能生成第一版，但用户常常不知道工具能力有多强，也不知道如何结构化迭代、测试、修 bug、打磨到客户期望。更进一步，做成生意还需要让其他人 care。这个判断会限制 Lovable 的自助式增长，也解释为什么它后续会补 ecosystem：产品生成之后，还要有 testing、analytics、distribution、payments、customer feedback 等能力。

“People do not always know how powerful Lovable is if you use it the right way. To make something lovable, to create a high-quality product, you need to keep testing it, find issues, and be structured in how you develop it. The first version usually does not hit the level of polish customers expect. For a business, you also need to make other people care about what you built.” —— Anton Osika, Building One, 2026-03-10

## Lovable 企业化来自员工把 home tool 带进 workplace，再反向要求 security 和 admin

Anton 说企业采用是双向的：人在家用 Lovable，带到公司；人在公司用 Lovable，又希望把它放进自己的创业工具箱。这个 bottom-up loop 使 enterprise security、admin 和治理成为自然产品需求。Lovable 的企业化先出现大量真实使用，再补安全、权限、管理功能，帮助公司放心把内部工具、finance/ops、PM/demo 等工作流放进来。

“People who use Lovable at home bring it into the workplace, and the other way around. If you use it at work, and you are thinking about starting a company, you want the tool you already know and like. So it became obvious: make the product enterprise-secure, and build the administrative functionality so enterprises can be successful with Lovable.” —— Anton Osika, Building One, 2026-03-10

## “vibe coding” 这个词太轻，Anton 更想表达经济工具和高质量软件生产

Anton 对 vibe coding 的不适感来自质量误读：这个词听起来 casual，好像生成出来的东西天然不严肃。但 Lovable 的真实目标是让任何角色创建 high quality software，并把这个能力变成 economic tool。这个语言问题会影响市场心智：如果用户把 Lovable 当玩具，它就停留在 prototype；如果把它理解成业务软件生产方式，企业和创业者才会把更重要的工作交给它。

“Vibe coding is polarizing because it sounds very casual, like it does not create high quality. But we are seeing real enterprises delivering things to end customers, founders building healthcare staffing platforms, and people using Lovable as an economic tool. The fundamental shift is that anyone can now create high-quality software.” —— Anton Osika, Building One, 2026-03-10
