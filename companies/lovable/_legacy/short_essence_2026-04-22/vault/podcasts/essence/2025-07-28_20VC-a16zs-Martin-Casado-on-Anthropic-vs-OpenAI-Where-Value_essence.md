# Martin Casado on AI Value Accrual and Lovable

## Martin 投 Lovable 的理由是 consumer brand 会在扩张期先吃 adoption

Martin 对 Lovable 的外部投资判断非常直接：AI 市场还在高速扩张，用户教育成本很低时，household name 会获得 disproportionate adoption。这个逻辑解释了 Lovable 为什么要重视 founder social、产品 love marks、community 和 launch noise。模型或功能差距如果不大，用户会选择他们听过、朋友在用、社交平台上不断出现的品牌。

“These markets are so large and growing so fast that we are seeing brand effects take place. If you become the household name, you get adoption because it does not require a lot of education or competitive discussion. Honestly, why did I do Lovable? For the same reason ChatGPT wins: I thought the consumer brand would win.” —— Martin Casado, 20VC, 2025-07-28

## Market expansion 阶段会奖励 household name，市场变慢后才进入细分竞争

Martin 用 cloud 市场类比解释 AI 应用的价值分布：市场扩张时，frontier 新用户只听到头部品牌，头部就持续获得增量；等增长放缓，用户才会细比功能、价格和替代。这个判断对 Lovable 很重要：现在的窗口期核心是尽快成为用户心中的默认 AI builder。品牌、速度和认知占位在扩张期的价值会被放大。

“Market expansion creates the dynamic where the frontier keeps expanding, and the first thing the frontier hears is the household names. The household names win. Once expansion slows, the frontier hears both names, and now you are in a discussion of which one to use.” —— Martin Casado, 20VC, 2025-07-28

## “每一层都有赢家”反驳了模型层吃掉应用层的简单叙事

Martin 反对 AI 投资里最常见的 zero-sum 思维：模型会不会吃掉 app，app 会不会没有 margin。他的判断是大市场里每一层都会有赢家，只是赢家会集中。对 Lovable 来说，问题不该停在“OpenAI 会不会做 app builder”，关键是 Lovable 能否在应用层建立足够强的 brand、workflow、domain data、community 和 distribution，让自己成为这一层的赢家。

“The one sin has been zero-sum thinking: will this layer get margin, will this layer get value? The answer has been yes. Every layer has gotten value, and every layer has winners. These markets are so large and growing so fast that brand effects take place.” —— Martin Casado, 20VC, 2025-07-28

## Application developers 仍有技术差异化空间，因为模型 scaling 不会均匀泛化

Martin 还给了应用层另一条防御路径：不同模型 scaling approach 不会对所有任务均匀提升，应用公司可以针对自己的领域做模型或 workflow 优化。Lovable 如果只调通用模型，会被模型厂和竞品挤压；如果它能积累 prompt-to-app、debug、UI、deploy、workflow、user intent 的专门数据和系统，应用层会出现自己的 technical differentiation。

“A lot of approaches to model scaling do not generalize. If I want to be much better at coding, I may not be as good at something else. This gives room for application developers to build their own models that serve areas the large models are not focused on. There is technical leverage to differentiate.” —— Martin Casado, 20VC, 2025-07-28

## AI coding 把 coding 变得 pleasant again，但真正难的工作仍然没有被完全吃掉

Martin 对 Cursor/coding model 的评价给 Lovable 一个边界提醒：AI 让编程重新变得愉快，清掉环境、package、hosting 等大量摩擦，但它对真正困难的实验、产品判断和复杂研究工作帮助有限。Lovable 的长期价值也会来自清掉非技术人从 idea 到 product 的整条摩擦链，同时承认 business judgment、distribution 和 workflow design 仍需要人。

“AI has made coding pleasant again. You can focus on what you want and the logic, while AI tells you how to host it and which package to use. But the hard things remain hard. It is great for tests, visualization, documentation, and maintainable codebases, while real frontier work still needs experimentation and judgment.” —— Martin Casado, 20VC, 2025-07-28
