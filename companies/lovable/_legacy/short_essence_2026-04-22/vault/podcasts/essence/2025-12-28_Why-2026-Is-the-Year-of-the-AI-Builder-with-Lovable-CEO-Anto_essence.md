# Why 2026 Is the Year of the AI Builder with Lovable CEO Anton Osika

## 2025 的变化是从 skeptical developer 到 enterprise pull，Lovable 被拉进工作流基础设施

Anton 在 AI Daily Brief 里回看 2025，最重要的变化是企业态度从 skeptical 到主动问“如何接进生产和基础设施”。他提到 Microsoft、Uber 等企业使用 Lovable，问题已经从“这个玩具有用吗”变成“如何 bridge 到 existing production/workflow”。这说明 Lovable 的 enterprise motion 很大程度是被 bottom-up usage 拉出来的，早期并非靠 sales push 硬推出来。

“Most people were skeptics at the end of 2024. Then people tried Lovable and very quickly got the wow aha moment: they could move much faster and do things humans could not do while building a first version. Now Lovable is being pulled into enterprises like Microsoft and Uber, and they ask how to bridge this into what they already have in production and whether they should use Lovable as infrastructure.” —— Anton Osika, The AI Daily Brief, 2025-12-28

## 用户演进从 early adopter agency 到 mainstream，再到公司重建 workflow

Anton 对用户结构演进的描述很适合写 Lovable 的 TAM：早期用户是技术倾向但非正式工程师的人，帮客户交付项目；中期进入 mainstream；最后 established companies 开始用 Lovable 重建 workflow，替换旧软件约束。这个路径比“nontechnical app builder”更大，因为它把 Lovable 从个人创作工具推向组织内部的软件再生产层。公司买 Lovable 的价值不止做原型，也包括重新定义某些工作流本身。

“In the beginning, Lovable was a super impressive tool for early adopters who were technically inclined but not formally trained engineers. They used it to deliver projects for clients. As it reached more mainstream users, more people built more use cases. The last big uptake is established companies realizing that software limitations shape how work gets done, so they use Lovable to reimagine workflows and replace what they used before with something custom for how their organization works.” —— Anton Osika, The AI Daily Brief, 2025-12-28

## Lovable 从 creation entry point 变成 load-bearing infrastructure，企业化的方向就自然出现

Anton 用了一个很重的词：load-bearing infrastructure。这个词说明 Lovable 的企业战略不止给 consumer tool 包一层 admin，它希望从 creation entry point 走向业务运行层。它要让企业内部用自然语言生成定制化 workflow、内部工具、营销站点和业务应用，同时叠加 AI 能力。这个方向也会让 Lovable 的产品需求变复杂：权限、治理、production handoff、security、integration 都会变成核心能力。

“There are hundreds of established companies using Lovable to reimagine workflows, replacing what they used before by building custom software for how their organization works and adding AI on top. This is going from Lovable as an entry point for creation to load-bearing infrastructure where you run your business on top of it. That is what we are leaning into as a platform.” —— Anton Osika, The AI Daily Brief, 2025-12-28

## Anton 最兴奋的是 personal software 变成小生意，Lovable 的供给端会不断长出微型创业者

Anton 对 2026 最兴奋的是 personal software 和 small business 的出现：用户从一个 idea 开始，做出产品，拿到客户，甚至形成收入。这个方向和 Lovable 的企业化并不矛盾：一边是大公司内部 workflow，一边是个人把 idea 变成经济活动。两条线共同指向一个更大的判断：软件生产成本下降后，需求端会被重新激活，过去不值得排工程资源的小问题会突然变成可执行项目。

“The exciting thing for me is when people have new ideas and create something new. The personal software trend turning into actual small businesses that make money is something I hear about every week: entrepreneurs passionately sharing what they built, especially when it is creative.” —— Anton Osika, The AI Daily Brief, 2025-12-28
