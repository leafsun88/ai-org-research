# The new AI growth playbook for 2026

## Elena 把旧增长 playbook 扔掉 60-70%，Lovable 的增长工作从优化变成发明新 loop

Elena 在 Lenny 这条长访谈里给出一个很硬的比例：她过去 15-20 年增长经验里只有 30-40% 可以迁移到 Lovable。AI 应用里的竞争不能靠同一条漏斗多 A/B 测几个点解决，因为每天都有新 entrant、模型能力和用户期待同时变化。Lovable 的增长 team 需要把 95% 精力放在 innovation，传统 optimization 只能占很小一部分。这个判断能解释为什么 Lovable 的增长组织更像 product/research 的延伸，传统 marketing function 的影子很弱。

“Only 30 to 40% of what I learned in the last 15 to 20 years of growth transfers here. Everybody is starting a vibe coding business, and to be ahead is not optimization of the problem, it is reinvention of the solution. In previous roles I might spend 5% innovating on growth; here I spend 95% innovating on growth and 5% optimizing.” —— Elena Verna, Lenny's Podcast, 2025-12-18

## Lovable 的 retention 要看订阅和 engagement 两条线，后者是付费留存的领先指标

Elena 对 retention 的拆法值得保留：subscriber retention 看付费用户续费和扩张，engagement retention 看用户有没有持续把任务交给 Lovable。AI 产品早期容易被 ARR 速度迷惑，但真正决定倍数的是 NDR 和 habit。Lovable 如果只是一次性 prototype 工具，留存会塌；如果用户把更多产品迭代、发布、营销、沟通动作放进来，engagement 才会变成 paid retention 的前置信号。

“I look at retention in two ways. Subscriber retention is how many paid subscribers we get, how many renew, and whether we can expand them. Engagement retention is also important because it is the leading indicator for what paid retention will look like. If you can show net dollar retention above 100%, that is a superpower for investors.” —— Elena Verna, Lenny's Podcast, 2025-12-18

## “If it is not lovable” 是内部质量语言，品牌被写成产品验收标准

Elena 讲 Lovable 的 brand 时，最有含量的是内部用语：如果一个 bug 或体验“not lovable”，团队会立刻修。这说明 Lovable 把品牌从外部传播变成内部质量系统，名字本身成为产品验收标准。AI 应用的体验高度不稳定，模型会出错、用户 prompt 会漂、生成结果会失败；这种情况下，brand 的核心不在广告预算，而在组织对“哪些体验不能放出去”的共同语言。

“We are trying to create absolutely lovable experiences. That is a mentality internally: if it is not lovable, we are not going to ship it. The best way to fix a bug at Lovable is to say, ‘this is not lovable,’ and everybody jumps on it. Sprints or no sprints, it is getting fixed right now.” —— Elena Verna, Lenny's Podcast, 2025-12-18

## 社区把探索行为变成 retention 和 word of mouth

Elena 把 community 放在 growth lever 里，因为用户在探索 AI 能力时需要互相学习、展示、模仿和获得正反馈。Lovable 的 Discord 有几十万成员，这让产品里的“我做出了一个东西”可以被社区放大成社交传播。对 AI builder 工具来说，社区的价值远超回答问题，它会把用户从单次生成拉进持续探索，帮助他们发现更多 use case，也帮助 Lovable 形成自传播内容池。

“Community is really important because you need to bring people together as they explore these capabilities and see what is possible. They can bounce off each other and help each other out. Community amplifies word of mouth, social posting, and retention. Ours started very early, runs on Discord, and has hundreds of thousands of members.” —— Elena Verna, Lenny's Podcast, 2025-12-18

## 每日 launch 保持市场噪音，每 1-2 个月做 T1 launch 制造 step function

Lovable 的 launch cadence 很有组织信息：每天都 launch，让市场持续感到产品在变好；每 1-2 个月再把功能 bundling 成 T1 launch，制造一段 narrative 和 step function。这个机制把产品节奏和增长节奏绑在一起，也让用户和社媒持续有话题。AI 应用如果三个月不让用户感到明显变强，很容易被替代；Lovable 用 continuous noise + periodic spike 来对抗这个 category 的高流动性。

“At Lovable, product launches are very different from any other company I have seen. We are committed to launching every day, so there is ongoing buzz: Lovable is evolving every single day. On top of that, every one to two months we make big T1 launches that bundle functionality around a story and a step-function change.” —— Elena Verna, Lenny's Podcast, 2025-12-18
