# His AI App Builder Went From $0 to $43M in 5 Months

## 43M ARR 阶段的 Lovable 已经把生成、分享、发布和早期分发连在一起

这条访谈的数字早于后续 100M/200M/350M 口径，但它保留了 Lovable 早期产品结构：用户输入自然语言，迭代几轮，点击分享和 launch，甚至通过 Lovable Launch 拿用户。最值得留的是当时 Lovable 已经意识到 app builder 不能停在生成代码，它必须把发布和早期曝光一起接住。

“We are making about $43 million per year. In the simplest form, you have an idea, describe it in plain text like you would to a human software engineer, evolve it through a few more prompts, and click to share it with friends or launch it on the Internet. Then maybe you get famous for building an AI product, or you start making money.” —— Anton Osika, Brett Malinowski, 2026-01-25

## 50,000-60,000 builds/day 显示供给爆炸，Lovable Launch 是把供给转成 demand 的第一层机制

Anton 提到每天 50,000-60,000 个新项目，并且用户会通过 Lovable Launch 发布。这说明 Lovable 已经面临“供给太多”的问题：生成软件变容易后，下一步一定是排序、发现、展示、分发。Lovable Launch 看起来像轻量 Product Hunt，但战略含义更大，它能把平台上生成的作品变成可见市场，进一步刺激用户继续 build。

“We have 50,000, maybe 60,000 builds per day. Some people who really want users tweet about them and use a publishing feature that is like our version of Product Hunt, called Lovable Launch. I hear founders say all my users came from Lovable Launch.” —— Anton Osika, Brett Malinowski, 2026-01-25

## Lovable 最自豪的能力是让非技术用户 99% 走到完成

Anton 对产品 secret sauce 的描述集中在 completion：非技术用户不能卡在 debug、部署、错误修复和“AI 差一点”的地方。Lovable 的技术难点不止调用模型，更要让用户一路走到可发布结果。这个能力会决定 retention，因为用户第一次被卡死后就会流失；如果系统能反复把用户带过 99% 的完成路径，它才有可能变成默认 builder。

“It comes down to details and understanding how users use the product, what they understand and do not understand. The most important thing is that even if you are not technical, you can get all the way to release product without getting stuck. Where AI falls short is often debugging and making things work 99% of the time all the way to completion.” —— Anton Osika, Brett Malinowski, 2026-01-25

## Anton 的成功用户画像是 marketing、product taste、AI 三者合一

这条访谈里 Anton 对“谁会赢”的判断和后续 20Growth 完全一致：AI 降低工程门槛后，拥有 distribution 和用户理解的人突然被放大。Lovable 的核心客户画像是懂产品、懂营销、能快速执行的人，单纯会 prompt 的价值有限。这个判断也解释了为什么 Lovable 的增长天然和社交媒体、creator、founder-led content、community 绑在一起。

“The people who will be most successful are those who understand marketing, understand what a good product looks like, and can use AI. If you have those three ingredients, move fast, and are ambitious, there are hundreds or thousands of opportunities.” —— Anton Osika, Brett Malinowski, 2026-01-25

## “say no” 是 Lovable 从探索转向聚焦后的组织纪律

Anton 在访谈后段讲了 say yes / say no 的节奏：早期要多试，找到方向后要系统地专注，持续做最重要的事。这是 Lovable 高速增长后最需要的组织纪律。AI 应用公司每天都有新模型、新 use case、新客户请求，如果没有强烈的 no，团队会被机会噪音拖散。Lovable 后续能否保住速度，很大程度取决于它能否把 founder 判断转成组织级 focus。

“Before you know what will be successful, you should try different things. But you should be systematic and say: this is the thing I believe in most, now I am going to see if customers care. Once you know what is working, focus and say no. Doing the important things all the way is crucial to deliver high quality.” —— Anton Osika, Brett Malinowski, 2026-01-25
