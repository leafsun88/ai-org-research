# He Built Lovable, the $6B AI Company Letting Anyone Create Software

## Lovable 的商业健康来自多类 builder，不只来自早期 AI hobbyist

Anton 在这条访谈里没有把收入爆发讲成投机增长。他说公司短期不以盈利为目标，但业务很健康，团队关注的是不同类型 builder：idea people、entrepreneurs、designers、PM、operations、finance、marketing 和 enterprise users。这个用户构成解释了 Lovable 的增长质量：它横跨开发者工具和 consumer app，让多个非工程角色获得软件生产能力。后续看 Lovable，重点要拆不同 builder cohort 的留存、扩张和付费意愿。

“I looked back at the financials for last year, and it is a very healthy business. We are not geared for profitability right now, but we are obsessing over all the different types of builders: idea people creating new businesses, entrepreneurs accelerating their business, designers, product managers, operations, finance, marketing, and large companies adopting Lovable.” —— Anton Osika, The AI Download, 2026-02-18

## Anton 选择服务 creative friends，这是 Lovable 简化界面的根

这条访谈再次确认 Lovable 的产品哲学：Anton 早期意识到 AI 会让工程师 10x，但他没有去做 Copilot 路线。他想到的是身边很多有创意的人无法把 idea 变成软件。Lovable 的界面之所以要极简，是因为它在替非技术人处理技术语言、debug、部署和架构决策。这个 founder insight 决定了 Lovable 不应该追求 IDE 式可调参数，至少在主界面上要持续隐藏复杂性。

“After playing with AI, it struck me one morning that I should not build something that accelerates software for engineers. I should build for my friends who are super creative, have good ideas, and have struggled to get them into reality. The interface has to be so simple, which is very hard, and it should just work.” —— Anton Osika, The AI Download, 2026-02-18

## “last piece of software” 的真正含义，是用户用问题生成工具

Anton 说 Lovable 在建“最后一块软件”，这句话容易被写成口号，但他的解释更具体：用户遇到任何软件问题，直接去 Lovable 描述问题，再生成解决它的软件。这个判断把 Lovable 放在 SaaS 市场的上游：它卖的是可变的软件生产能力，而非固定 workflow。风险也在这里：如果生成结果不能可靠到 production，它只是 prototype；如果能接住安全、数据、支付和运营，它会压缩许多长尾 SaaS。

“Since day one we have said we are building the last piece of software. What we mean is that you should not need to create new software by starting from scratch. You go to Lovable, describe whatever problem you want software to solve, and you get a piece of software that solves that problem.” —— Anton Osika, The AI Download, 2026-02-18

## 50,000-60,000 builds/day 和 Lovable Launch，把产品供给变成分发入口

Anton 提到 Lovable 每天有 50,000-60,000 个新项目，用户会通过 Lovable Launch 发布并拿到用户。这个数字和机制都值得留：Lovable 不只是生产软件，也开始承接软件发布后的曝光和早期用户获取。对很多小 builder 来说，product hunt 式入口会决定他们是否继续迭代。Lovable 如果能把 build、publish、share、feedback 串起来，就能从工具升级为 builder network。

“We have around 50,000 or 60,000 builds per day. Some people who want users tweet about what they built, and they also use a publish feature that is like our version of Product Hunt, called Lovable Launch. I hear founders say all their users came from Lovable Launch.” —— Anton Osika, The AI Download, 2026-02-18

## 成功用户需要 marketing、product taste 和 AI，Lovable 降低工程门槛后反而抬高判断力门槛

Anton 对谁会在未来几年赢的判断很直接：会 marketing、懂好产品、能用 AI、行动快、有野心的人。Lovable 降低了 build 门槛，但没有替用户解决“做什么值得做、如何让人 care、如何分发”的判断。这个 insight 很重要，因为它限制了 Lovable 的 TAM 转化率：生成工具把供给打开，真正能变成业务的用户仍需要 taste、distribution 和市场理解。

“The people who will be most successful in the coming years are those who understand marketing, understand what a good product looks like, and can use AI. If you have those three ingredients, move fast, and are ambitious, there are hundreds or thousands of opportunities to get rich on the Internet.” —— Anton Osika, The AI Download, 2026-02-18
