# #487 - VO - Anton Osika - Lovable - Internet, Business, and AI

## Anton 把 Lovable 放在“build the thing that builds things”这个更高层

这条长访谈里，Anton 把个人经历、上一家公司和 Lovable 接在一起：他早期从物理转向软件，是因为想更直接地 build things；后来意识到更大的问题是让更多人能 build high-quality software products；最后 Lovable 成了“building the thing that builds things”。这个表达非常适合报告主线：Lovable 的业务不是单一 app builder，而是生产软件的生产工具。它的组织也必须围绕这个二阶任务配置，既懂产品，也懂工程，也懂人如何表达需求。

“For the last ten years, I've been part of building startups, software products, and teams that build products. The ultimate problem to solve, from my experience, is to let more people do what has been very difficult: create a fully working, high-quality software product. Over the last few decades, software has been eating the world, but now it's possible for the 99% who don't know how to code at all to create the software. So the full circle is me realizing you should build things, and now building the thing that builds things is what Lovable is.” —— Anton Osika, Génération Do It Yourself, 2025-08-17

## Lovable 选择做 user experience 层，是因为 foundation model 竞争太贵也太拥挤

Anton 对创业位置的判断很清楚：不要和 OpenAI、Anthropic、Google 在模型层正面对打，要拥有用户体验和 interface，使用最好的模型，未来再叠加自己的数据。这个判断是 Lovable 的战略地基。它不是低技术路线，反而要求组织把模型能力转译成稳定产品能力：prompt、文件、UI、database、部署、安全、支付、协作都要被产品层吃掉。

“You shouldn't play in that very competitive space. You should play in the space of owning what the user experiences, owning the interface, using the best model, and over time maybe adding some of your own data on top of those models. You will not compete with Anthropic or OpenAI; it's too expensive and too much investment already. But you can own the next layer, the user layer.” —— Anton Osika, Génération Do It Yourself, 2025-08-17

## Lovable 的长期界面还没有定型，prompt 只是当前最有效的起点

Anton 反复说 Lovable 要找的是“new type of interface”。现在是 prompt/chat，但他并没有把 prompt 当终局。这个认知很重要：很多 AI 应用会被当前 UI 锁死，Lovable 更像是在通过真实使用持续发现未来软件创造界面。组织上，这要求团队保持 interface imagination，不只是优化今天的 chat box。它解释了为什么 Lovable 需要 technical product engineers，而不是传统 PM + engineer 分工。

“The more pressing and valuable problem is not making people incrementally more productive, but letting 99% of people go from not being able to build at all to being able to use AI with a new type of way to build products, to go zero to one. It is a new type of interface, and we have to figure out exactly what the best interface is. Now we have a new interface with Lovable, but I think there are many iterations left to get to perfection.” —— Anton Osika, Génération Do It Yourself, 2025-08-17

## Lovable 的协作场景正在从个人 builder 走向“demo, not memo”

Anton 在这条访谈里很早讲到了大公司协作场景：有产品想法的人不再写 PPT 或 PRD，而是几分钟做出一个可体验 prototype，拿给客户、同事、团队试。这个机制改变的是企业内部需求流：工程资源只在方向更确定后介入，前面的 idea exploration 由 Lovable 承接。它也解释了 Lovable 为什么不是单纯低端创业工具，反而会进入 enterprise workflow。

“If you have a product and a lot of ideas, you can discuss the ideas or spend a few minutes and show what this idea would look like. You can discuss it, show what it will look like, try it on clients or prospects, and see if it works. Over the coming weeks we're making that much more seamless: how do you collaborate, how do you see what others in your team are proposing to change in the product.” —— Anton Osika, Génération Do It Yourself, 2025-08-17

“There are many large companies where people want to propose what to do next in the product or internal processes by building out a working prototype, so they can feel whether this is actually where the product should go before committing engineering resources.” —— Anton Osika, Génération Do It Yourself, 2025-08-17

## Growth engineer 是 Lovable 把产品生命周期继续往后吃的信号

Anton 讲到“growth engineer”时，已经把 Lovable 想成一个会主动跑实验、提升转化、提出发布建议的系统。这不是 coding assistant，而是产品运营角色的自动化雏形。它会把 Lovable 从 build tool 推向 operating layer：用户不只生成产品，还让 AI 帮忙观察、实验、优化。这个方向一旦成立，Lovable 的收入不再只和生成次数相关，而和用户持续经营业务绑定。

“What will happen soon is that you will be able to have this AI, maybe call it the growth engineer, that tells you when you wake up: hey, I just ran an A/B test and you have a 4% conversion increase. Do you want to look at my work, or should I just go and publish it? This is going to happen soon.” —— Anton Osika, Génération Do It Yourself, 2025-08-17

## Lovable 的 $90M 到 $100M ARR 叙事强调 self-serve，而不是 sales-led 复制

Anton 在 8 月已经说 Lovable launch 七个月后接近 $90M ARR，播客发布时会到 $100M。更重要的是，他把增长归因为 self-serve，而不是 sales motion。这个数据和机制要一起看：如果增长主要来自自助使用和 word of mouth，Lovable 的组织早期就可以保持 product-led、高速度、低销售依赖；但随着 enterprise pull 增强，后续挑战是补 GTM 能力又不把公司节奏改成传统销售机器。

“At the last company where I was cofounder, it was a sales-driven distribution motion. With Lovable, it has been a completely self-served product. We are seven months after launch and we're at $90 million ARR. When we release this podcast, it will be $100 million.” —— Anton Osika, Génération Do It Yourself, 2025-08-17

## Anton 对竞争的回答是回到用户，而不是盯 OpenAI 和 Anthropic 的路线图

被问到 OpenAI、Anthropic、Google 是否会打 Lovable，Anton 的回答是“想竞争对手不如想怎么让自己的产品更好”。这不是回避竞争，而是 AI 应用层的一个有效纪律：基础模型公司一定会扩边界，创业公司能控制的是 user experience、workflow、数据、品牌和客户成功。Lovable 如果过度被上游动态牵引，就会失去自己的 product rhythm。

“Every time I think about competitors, I would spend my time better thinking about how to make our product better, understand my users, and help them succeed. Not thinking about them.” —— Anton Osika, Génération Do It Yourself, 2025-08-17

## Anton 的模型判断是“应用层拥有用户，模型层提供能力”

这条访谈对 Lovable 的战略边界总结得很清楚：模型公司会很强，但应用层要拥有第一步用户、拥有体验、拥有 workflow。Lovable 不是假装自己能训练出更好模型，而是把“第一触点”和“产品生命周期”握住。这个判断对投资分析很关键：Lovable 的价值不取决于 Claude 是否永远第一，而取决于它能不能让多个模型能力在自己平台里变成更高层的工作流。

“The next layer is the user in the first step that is built. It's not about competing with the foundation labs. It's about owning what the user experiences and making the model useful inside a product.” —— Anton Osika, Génération Do It Yourself, 2025-08-17
