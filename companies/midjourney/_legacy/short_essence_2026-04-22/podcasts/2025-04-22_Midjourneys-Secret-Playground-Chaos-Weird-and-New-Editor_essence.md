# Midjourney's Secret Playground: Chaos, Weird, and New Editor

## Drip release 把 Midjourney 社区重新点燃，产品节奏开始替代单次大版本声量

这期紧接 V7 之后，最有用的观察是社区情绪的变化。主持人提到，几周前 Midjourney 在社媒上有点冷，但新功能连续掉落后，用户又开始测试、截图、讨论、复盘参数。Midjourney 的 release cadence 本身正在成为产品机制：用持续掉落的小功能、参数和编辑能力替代一年一次的大版本等待，让社区边玩边学习。对一个小团队来说，这种节奏能用社区热度放大测试能力，也能缓解模型大版本被竞品对比时的压力。

“A lot has dropped already, and more is queued up. This dripping system feels better than waiting forever for the next giant V7 moment.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-22

“Five or six weeks ago, Twitter felt quiet around Midjourney. Constant new stuff makes people test again, talk again, and share results again.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-22

“The cadence matters. Every new parameter or editor feature gives the community a reason to return to the model.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-22

## Weird 和 chaos 是探索 Midjourney latent space 的工具

主持人对 weird/chaos 的解释非常贴近 Midjourney 的独特性：这些参数的核心用途偏探索，帮助用户离开熟悉审美，挖到自己 prompt 不出来的视觉方向。它们更像 exploration knobs。Midjourney 的优势长期在审美 surprise，weird/chaos 让用户把这种 surprise 系统化：先用高变异参数探索，再把有价值的结果做成 style reference 或 mood board。这个机制说明 Midjourney 的产品哲学并不完全是“按指令执行”，它仍保留了大量共同创作和偶然性。

“Weird and chaos give you things you would not normally prompt. They are not pure control tools; they help you explore the Midjourney landscape.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-22

“I use weird almost like mining for style refs. Find something I would never have written, then bring it back as a style reference or mood board.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-22

“If personalization is on, the model still pulls toward your profile. Turn it off and the variety opens up much more.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-22

## Editor 开始把 Midjourney 从抽卡工具推向后期制作工具

这期对新 editor 的讨论显示，Midjourney 正在补从生成到编辑的工作流缺口。retexture、resize、paint、smart select、layers 这些能力单独看都像小功能，但合在一起，它们让用户可以在已有图像上做局部修正、质感重塑和结构调整。主持人也很清醒地说，它还处于早期，但 optionality 很重要。对 Midjourney 来说，编辑器是从“生成漂亮图片”走向“可交付资产”的必经步骤。

“Editor, retexture, resize, paint, smart select and layers are starting to live together. You can see the shape of a fuller workflow.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-22

“Layers and retexture need to work together if you want to smooth things out. It is not just generate and pray anymore.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-22

“It is not yet a groundbreaking editor, but the optionality matters. Coming from Photoshop, some of these moves already feel wild.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-22

## V7 早期争议被 ChatGPT image 更新放大，Midjourney 的用户评估需要自己的尺度

主持人指出，V7 发布刚好撞上 ChatGPT image update，导致很多用户用 prompt adherence、文字理解和易用性来快速比较两者。这种比较有意义，但也容易让 Midjourney 的差异被遮住：Midjourney 不只是听话模型，它长期押的是审美、风格探索和创作者控制感。V7 是新架构，需要用户重新学习参数和 workflow。如果只跑几个 prompt，就会把一个需要探索的 creative model 误读成一个没有完全执行指令的 assistant。

“V7 is a new architecture; you have to figure it out. ChatGPT image updates came right before it, so people judged it with a few prompts and moved on.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-22

“If you only test prompt adherence, you miss what Midjourney is good at. The model is about aesthetics, variation, and creative direction too.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-22

“People wanted instant certainty. Midjourney often needs exploration before the value shows up.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-22

## Video model 的延迟说明 Midjourney 宁愿等 extraordinary，也不愿用半成品抢发布

这期里提到视频模型训练接近尾声，但发布时间仍可能延后。主持人的解读是，如果要等一个 extraordinary 的版本，他们宁愿等。这个信号符合 Midjourney 一贯的产品气质：它不喜欢用功能清单追竞品，更愿意等模型体验达到某种审美阈值再放出来。风险是节奏显得慢，尤其 Runway、Google、OpenAI 等都在视频上快速迭代；优势是品牌不会被大量中庸功能稀释。Midjourney 的组织约束也在这里显现：小团队无法所有方向同时极快，只能押体验阈值。

“The video model training is expected near the end of the month, but release could still be one or two months out.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-22

“If waiting means something extraordinary, I would rather wait. Midjourney should not ship video just to say it shipped video.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-22

“The risk is that competitors keep moving. The upside is that the Midjourney brand still means quality and surprise.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-22
