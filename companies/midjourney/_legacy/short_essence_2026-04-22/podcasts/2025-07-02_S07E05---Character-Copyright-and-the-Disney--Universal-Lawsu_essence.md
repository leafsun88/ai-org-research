# S07E05 - Character Copyright and the Disney / Universal Lawsuit

## 角色版权是 Midjourney 案子的难点，也是 Disney 最容易让法官看懂的地方

这期法律播客比普通新闻更有深度，因为它把角色版权的模糊性讲清楚了。美国版权法并没有把“角色”写成一个简单、明确的对象，很多规则来自法院判例；但在 Disney/Universal 起诉 Midjourney 时，原告会拿 side-by-side 输出图像证明生成结果就是 Darth Vader、Minions、Lightning McQueen 等角色。Midjourney 可以围绕 fair use、角色可版权性、用户行为和模型责任展开防御，但视觉相似性会让原告叙事非常强。这个案子会直接审视模型能否商品化复刻强 IP 角色。

“Character copyright is not cleanly written into the Copyright Act. A lot of it is judge-made, which makes the doctrine messy.” —— Lawyer's Guide, 2025-07-02

“The images in the complaint are clearly recognizable characters. If you see Vader, Minions or Lightning McQueen, non-infringement becomes hard to sell.” —— Lawyer's Guide, 2025-07-02

“Midjourney still has defenses: fair use, limits of character copyright, user conduct, and how outputs are generated. But the visuals make Disney's story easy to understand.” —— Lawyer's Guide, 2025-07-02

## “机器生成”不能自动切断平台责任，训练和输出之间会被法院重新连接

播客里一个有用的类比是：如果你训练一只狗去攻击，狗实际咬人时不能完全说自己没有责任。这个说法虽然粗糙，但能解释法院可能如何看 AI 模型：模型输出不是人类逐字复制，却可能是平台通过训练、产品设计和默认能力塑造出来的结果。Midjourney 的风险在于，它既提供模型，也设计 prompt 入口、默认参数、社区示例和输出展示；如果模型反复输出受保护角色，平台很难只把责任推给用户或“机器偶然性”。

“If you train a dog to attack, you cannot just blame the dog when it attacks. Courts may ask what the platform trained and enabled the system to do.” —— Lawyer's Guide, 2025-07-02

“The defense cannot simply be that a machine produced it. The platform built the model, shaped the interface, and made the output commercially useful.” —— Lawyer's Guide, 2025-07-02

“The harder the product leans into recognizable characters, the harder it is to separate training choices from output behavior.” —— Lawyer's Guide, 2025-07-02

## Fair use 的战场会落在市场伤害，fan art 逻辑对商业平台不够安全

这期讨论把 fair use 的一个关键区分讲得很细：普通人在家画 fan art 和一个商业平台大规模生成 IP 角色，不会被法院同等看待。个人创作可能市场伤害很小，但 Midjourney 的输出被上传、传播、用于注意力经济、甚至可能进入商业场景。原告会说这些图像替代授权插画、品牌资产或用户生成内容市场；Midjourney 会说用户生成图像不会让人少看电影、少买周边。真正的分歧在于，AI 图像平台有没有制造一个未经许可的平行角色资产市场。

“A person drawing fan art at home is one thing. A commercial platform letting millions create and share those images is a different market story.” —— Lawyer's Guide, 2025-07-02

“Midjourney can argue no one stops watching Andor because someone generated Darth Vader. But online attention and commercial use complicate the harm analysis.” —— Lawyer's Guide, 2025-07-02

“The market question is not only direct substitution. It is whether the platform creates an unlicensed market for character assets.” —— Lawyer's Guide, 2025-07-02

## 这个案子大概率会变成商业和解，因为双方都需要可交易规则

律师播客对结果的判断偏现实：Disney 的 complaint 很强，Midjourney 也有防御，但真正走到终局判决会对双方都有风险。Disney 想保护 IP，但也不一定想彻底杀死 AI 创作工具；Midjourney 想守住 fair use 和开放生成，但也需要进入更可商业化的专业市场。和解、授权、过滤、收入分成和品牌 guardrails 可能比单纯输赢更重要。这个判断也符合 AI 版权案件的一般方向：最终很可能形成新的许可和平台责任结构。

“The complaint looks good for Disney. The side-by-side images are effective, and the story is easy to tell.” —— Lawyer's Guide, 2025-07-02

“Midjourney has viable defenses. Whether those defenses win is different from whether the case should settle.” —— Lawyer's Guide, 2025-07-02

“A business resolution may make more sense: rules, filters, licensing, and a path where creators and platforms can keep operating.” —— Lawyer's Guide, 2025-07-02

## Midjourney 的专业化会被版权治理倒逼，不再能只靠 creative freedom 叙事

这期法律分析对公司战略的含义很直接：版权治理将成为 Midjourney 从创作者玩具走向专业平台的门槛。它可以继续服务个人探索和美学实验，但一旦进入广告、品牌、影视、游戏、企业视觉生产，客户就会要求更清晰的权利边界。Midjourney 必须在 freedom 和 safety 之间设计产品层：哪些输出允许，哪些需要授权，哪些场景给企业 indemnity，哪些角色和品牌必须拒绝。法律会直接定义 Midjourney 可进入的商业市场。

“Creative freedom is powerful, but professional users need certainty. They need to know what rights they have and what risks they carry.” —— Lawyer's Guide, 2025-07-02

“The lawsuit pressures Midjourney to build governance into the product, not only rely on users to behave.” —— Lawyer's Guide, 2025-07-02

“If image generation becomes part of advertising, film, games and brand production, copyright controls become product features.” —— Lawyer's Guide, 2025-07-02
