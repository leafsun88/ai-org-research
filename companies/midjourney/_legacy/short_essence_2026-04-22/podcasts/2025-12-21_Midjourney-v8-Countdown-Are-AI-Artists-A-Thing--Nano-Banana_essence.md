# Midjourney v8 Countdown, Are AI Artists A Thing? Nano Banana

## Style creator 暗示 Midjourney 正在把“个人风格”产品化

这期最有价值的信号是 style creator。主持人提到它在 alpha.midjourney.com 上软启动，甚至可能比 V8 本身更快被用户感知。Midjourney 的模型升级当然重要，但 style creator 说明公司正在把用户的风格资产产品化：用户不只是 prompt 一张图，而是构建可复用的 aesthetic profile。这个方向和 V7 personalization、style reference、mood board 是一条线，目标是让 Midjourney 不再只生产单张漂亮图片，而是帮助用户形成连续、可识别、可复用的视觉语言。

“Style creator is live on alpha, almost like a soft launch. It feels like Midjourney is putting style systems in front of users before the main V8 story lands.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-12-21

“V8 may be delayed, but style creator could be the main thing people actually use first.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-12-21

“The direction is clear: Midjourney wants users to build a style they can return to, not just generate one-off images.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-12-21

## “AI artist” 争议背后真正的问题，是单张图能力已经不稀缺，系列化 vision 才稀缺

主持人讨论 “AI artists” 时，重点放在一个更具体的问题：生成大量图片已经变得廉价，真正稀缺的是能否把这些图组织成一套 coherent story。很多用户都有几十万张生成图，单图惊艳已经不能区分能力；创作者的价值转向持续审美、叙事、选择和长期 vision。这个判断很适合放进 Midjourney 分析：当模型越来越强，Midjourney 需要帮助用户从“抽到好图”升级成“建立作品系统”，否则它会停留在图像老虎机阶段。

“Everyone has hundreds of thousands of images now. The work becomes how to tell a cohesive bigger story with them.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-12-21

“The word artist is charged. Some people use Midjourney for business assets and do not care about that label at all.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-12-21

“Some creators do have a recognizable style. They keep going, refine it, and eventually it becomes their vision.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-12-21

## Midjourney 在 realistic aesthetic 上仍强，但 ChatGPT 和 Nano Banana 抢走了易用性与大众入口

这期对竞争格局的描述很清楚：主持人仍认为 Midjourney 在 realistic aesthetic 上强于 ChatGPT image 和 Nano Banana，但后者拥有更大的易用性、对话入口和用户规模。Midjourney 的主要风险来自大众用户对“够好、方便、免费或集成”的偏好，这可能压过对最高审美的追求。专业用户会为质量付费，普通用户可能留在 ChatGPT、Google 或社交平台里。Midjourney 的长期定位会更像 premium creative engine。

“ChatGPT and Nano Banana still do not feel close on realistic aesthetic, but they have accessibility and a much larger surface area.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-12-21

“For casual users, good enough and already inside the tool they use every day can beat a better standalone image product.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-12-21

“Professionals may stay with Midjourney for quality. The mass market will keep testing whatever is easiest.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-12-21

## Video 和 world model 讨论会把 Midjourney 带进更高成本、更高技术密度的战场

主持人提到视频模型、world model 和 2026 的行业讨论。这对 Midjourney 很关键：图像生成已经进入多家竞争，视频和世界模型会把算力、数据、物理一致性和长序列控制的要求大幅提高。Midjourney 的审美优势能否迁移到视频，不是确定答案。它需要在高质量、风格、角色一致性、可编辑性和成本之间找到新平衡。V8 和后续视频模型不仅是功能升级，也会考验小团队能否继续在更重的技术战场里保持产品品味。

“Video models trained at scale start to become world models. In 2026, that conversation is going to get louder.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-12-21

“The question is whether Midjourney's aesthetic advantage can carry into video, where consistency, physics and editing matter much more.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-12-21

“Image quality got them here. The next frontier will test whether the same taste and product judgment can survive a much harder medium.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-12-21
