# Meta's Midjourney Deal, Oz Remixed Premiere, AI & Runway Gaming

## Meta 授权 Midjourney 的信号，是大平台缺少自己的高审美视觉模型

这期材料最重要的点很短，但战略含量高：Meta 与 Midjourney 达成 licensing deal，意味着大平台并不一定能靠内部模型快速复制 Midjourney 的审美资产。Meta 有分发、社交图谱、广告客户和海量内容场景，但如果它需要 Midjourney 的 aesthetic tech，说明图像生成的差异化不只在算力和基础模型，也在长期审美调参、社区反馈和产品品味。Midjourney 作为独立公司，如果能把审美模型授权给大平台，它的价值就不只是 C 端订阅，而是成为社交和广告平台的视觉引擎。

“Meta, Instagram and WhatsApp are licensing Midjourney technology. That says a lot: even giant platforms want Midjourney's aesthetic layer.” —— AI Film News, 2025-08-26

“Meta has distribution and users, but Midjourney has the vibe. That visual feel is what people are trying to borrow.” —— AI Film News, 2025-08-26

“The deal points to a future where creative effects, ads and image tools inside social platforms may run on Midjourney DNA.” —— AI Film News, 2025-08-26

## Midjourney 的审美会被平台化放大，也会带来同质化风险

Meta 合作的另一面，是 Midjourney 的风格可能被放大到数十亿用户级别。对公司来说，这是极强分发；对创作生态来说，也可能让“Midjourney look”进一步泛滥。播客提到，如果你喜欢这种 look，这当然好；但当大平台内置同一套视觉美学，原创性和差异化会更难。这个风险也会反过来影响 Midjourney：它必须不断推出 personalization、style reference、style creator 和更细控制能力，让用户不只得到漂亮图，还能得到属于自己的视觉语言。

“If you like the Midjourney look, this is exciting. But if billions of users get the same aesthetic baked in, originality gets trickier.” —— AI Film News, 2025-08-26

“The platform can make beautiful images easier for everyone, but it can also make everything feel like it came from the same place.” —— AI Film News, 2025-08-26

“That is why personalization and style control matter. Midjourney has to help people make images that do not all collapse into one default vibe.” —— AI Film News, 2025-08-26

## 这笔合作把 Midjourney 从创作者工具推向基础设施，但也会削弱它的直接用户关系

Meta 合作对 Midjourney 是双刃剑。它证明 Midjourney 的模型能力可以作为平台基础设施出售，可能带来高毛利授权收入和更大影响力；但如果用户在 Instagram、WhatsApp 或 Meta 广告工具里用到 Midjourney 能力，却不再进入 Midjourney 自己的产品，Midjourney 的直接用户关系和社区学习循环会被稀释。长期看，Midjourney 需要在 licensing 和 owned interface 之间保持平衡：既让大平台放大模型能力，又不能把自己的品牌、社区和数据飞轮完全交出去。

“Licensing gives Midjourney distribution it could never build alone. But it also means some users may experience the technology without becoming Midjourney users.” —— AI Film News, 2025-08-26

“The strategic question is whether Midjourney becomes a creative infrastructure layer while still keeping its own community and product alive.” —— AI Film News, 2025-08-26

“If Meta owns the user relationship and Midjourney owns the aesthetic engine, the value split becomes the thing to watch.” —— AI Film News, 2025-08-26
