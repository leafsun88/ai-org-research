# Nick from 'Midjourney for Creatives': Unlocking the full power of generative AI art via Midjourney

## Midjourney 把 10x creative 变成真实职业路径，关键在工具堆叠而非单点出图

这期材料的核心价值，是从 power user 角度解释 Midjourney 怎样超出单点 image toy。主持人开场把 “10x developer” 类比成 “10x creative”，Nick 的工作方式则说明这种杠杆来自工具组合：Midjourney 负责视觉想象，Runway/ElevenLabs/ChatGPT 等工具接住视频、声音、叙事和音乐。对创作者来说，AI 的能力会把原本需要一个小团队完成的概念、视觉、声音、短片、营销资产压缩到一个人的 workflow。Midjourney 在其中的位置，是最强的视觉起点和审美引擎。

“We talk about the 10x developer; with these tools, there is a 10x creative too. One person can do so much when Midjourney, ElevenLabs, Runway and ChatGPT start stacking together.” —— Probably Something, 2023-11-06

“Midjourney gets you from an idea in your head to an actual thing almost instantly. Once that first image exists, the rest of the creative stack has something to build on.” —— Nick, Probably Something, 2023-11-06

“The power is not one magic prompt. It is learning how these systems connect, where each one is strong, and how to move an idea through the stack.” —— Nick, Probably Something, 2023-11-06

## Midjourney 教育生态来自用户把自己的探索过程开源出来

Nick 的课程从 Twitter 上公开分享 prompt 过程自然长出来，比传统软件培训更贴近 Midjourney 的使用方式。这个细节说明 Midjourney 的早期生态依赖 power users 做 community teaching：他们不断公开过程、失败、参数、风格和练习题，让新用户更快越过冷启动。Midjourney 官方团队很小，无法像 Adobe 那样大规模生产教程和认证体系；社区里的 Nick 们就承担了这部分功能。对平台来说，这类外部教育者不仅带来获客，也把审美和使用习惯标准化，间接塑造产品文化。

“The course was a natural extension of what I was already doing on Twitter. I was posting educational tutorials and open-sourcing my process while experimenting with Midjourney.” —— Nick, Probably Something, 2023-11-06

“Maven came to me during their AI push and asked whether I wanted to turn the content into a course. At first I was hesitant, because I did not know if I wanted to be pigeonholed as an education person.” —— Nick, Probably Something, 2023-11-06

“The exercises matter because people need reps. Midjourney is visual, but prompting is still a craft, and people learn faster when they can practice against a concrete task.” —— Nick, Probably Something, 2023-11-06

## Nick 对 David Holz 的信任来自 Leap Motion 时代，Midjourney 的品牌有创始人技术信誉加成

Nick 被问到为什么更喜欢 Midjourney 时，第一反应先落在 David Holz 的技术信誉。他早年做 VR 时见过 Leap Motion 的手部追踪能力，认为 David 带小团队做出了大公司做不到的东西。这种创始人过往能力会进入用户和生态伙伴的信任判断：Midjourney 更像一个创始人长期追求人机交互、想象力和创作工具的连续项目。这个信任降低了 power user 对平台不确定性的担心，也强化了 Midjourney 的“maker-led lab”形象。

“I knew David Holz from the VR world, when he was doing Leap Motion. I had a VR company too, and what they built in hand tracking was incredible.” —— Nick, Probably Something, 2023-11-06

“They were a startup, but they built things that the Metas and Googles could not come close to at the time. That gave me trust in him as an entrepreneur and technologist.” —— Nick, Probably Something, 2023-11-06

“Then the Midjourney image quality speaks for itself. The aesthetics are the reason people keep coming back.” —— Nick, Probably Something, 2023-11-06

## AI artist 这个身份会有争议，但 Midjourney 的真实增量是让人类故事重新进入机器输出

Nick 对 “AI artist” 这个词很谨慎。他承认机器完成了大量 heavy lifting，所以单纯把 AI 输出等同于传统艺术会引发争议；但他也指出，人类仍然可以通过故事、策展、系列化和叙事把机器输出转成作品。这对 Midjourney 的长期定位很关键：它不一定要争夺“谁才是艺术家”的定义权，更重要的是成为一种让更多人表达世界观和审美的 production layer。用户真正能带来的差异，不在单张图，而在一组作品背后的 narrative、taste 和 curation。

“I do not necessarily call myself an AI artist. When you put AI in front of it, you have to acknowledge that the machine is doing a lot of the heavy lifting.” —— Nick, Probably Something, 2023-11-06

“The human part is the story, the curation, the authentic addition to the narrative. That is where it can become something beautiful.” —— Nick, Probably Something, 2023-11-06

“A single image can be impressive, but a collection with a point of view is where the creator starts to show up.” —— Nick, Probably Something, 2023-11-06

## Midjourney 的商业扩散路径会先经过创意人群，再进入营销、品牌和内容生产

这期播客虽然没有给出 Midjourney 官方 GTM，但用户侧已经能看到扩散路径：创意人群先把它当实验室，再把生成图用于品牌视觉、营销素材、叙事资产和多媒体项目。Nick 的角色介于教育者、创作者和 consultant 之间，这正是 Midjourney 早期商业生态的一部分。它不需要官方先搭很重的销售组织，就能通过高水平用户把能力带进客户项目。这个生态越成熟，Midjourney 的产品就越像一个基础创作层，围绕它会长出课程、模板、咨询、插件、工作流和创作者服务。

“People start by playing, but very quickly they ask how this can become part of their creative business. The use case moves from image generation to campaign, brand and story.” —— Nick, Probably Something, 2023-11-06

“When students learn Midjourney, they are not only learning prompts. They are learning how to think visually, how to iterate, and how to make outputs useful.” —— Nick, Probably Something, 2023-11-06

“The opportunity is bigger when a creator can connect the image to the rest of the project: video, voice, music, copy, website, marketing.” —— Nick, Probably Something, 2023-11-06
