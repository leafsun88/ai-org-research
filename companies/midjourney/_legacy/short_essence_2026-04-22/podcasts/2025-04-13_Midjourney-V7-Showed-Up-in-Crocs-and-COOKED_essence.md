# Midjourney V7 Showed Up in Crocs and COOKED

## V7 的第一反应被低估，因为 Midjourney 用户需要重新学习模型边界

这期用户评测最有价值的地方，是它把 V7 当作一套新架构带来的重新学习过程。播客主持人反复提到，新模型发布后，如果只用老 prompt 跑几张图就下判断，很容易误判；真正的用户评测要回到 drawing board，把旧素材、参数、风格、prompt habit 全部重新测试。Midjourney 的产品速度不只取决于模型发布，也取决于用户社区能否快速形成新模型的使用知识。V7 早期争议本质上是模型能力、用户习惯和竞品比较节奏之间的错位。

“Every new Midjourney model makes me go back to old prompts. Images that felt revolutionary before suddenly look like old assets.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-13

“You cannot judge V7 with a few prompts and walk away. You have to break enough images, test enough variables, and learn what this architecture wants from you.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-13

“A lot of incremental changes are not announced. You only discover them when you go back to the drawing board and test parameters, features and old workflows again.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-13

## Personalization 从附加功能变成默认路径，Midjourney 正在把 taste 变成产品资产

V7 的关键变化之一是 personalization 变得更重要。主持人提到，自己 rank 了大量图片后，手、颜色、构图和整体结果都明显更接近个人偏好。这个机制很像把用户的 taste 变成模型前置条件：用户一边输入 prompt，一边持续训练一个个性化审美层。对 Midjourney 来说，这是一种很强的 retention 机制，用户投入越多偏好数据，越难迁移到另一个工具；对业务来说，personalization 也可能成为未来专业用户和团队工作流的基础。

“V7 feels like a base model meant to be built on and customized. Personalization is no longer a side thing; it is becoming the default way to use it.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-13

“I ranked around two hundred images, and the hands, the colors and the composition started to feel immediately closer to my taste.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-13

“The more you rank, the more the model feels like it understands your taste. That changes how you prompt, because you are no longer talking to a neutral model.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-13

## V7 让 stylize 的甜点区间下移，说明新模型在默认审美上更强

主持人提到，以前会把 stylize 推到很高，现在 V7 在更低区间已经能得到稳定、漂亮、有控制感的结果。这个细节看似参数经验，实则说明模型默认审美能力提高后，用户不再需要用极端 stylize 来强行拉效果。对专业用户来说，参数甜点区间的变化会直接影响 workflow：旧参数很难直接迁移，用户必须重新建立新模型的控制手感。Midjourney 每次模型升级都在改变用户的“驾驶方式”，这也是它需要强社区学习和大量教程生态的原因。

“I used to push stylize very high, seven or eight hundred sometimes. With V7, I am back around one-fifty, two hundred, three hundred and it feels steadier.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-13

“The model has more aesthetic weight on its own. You do not need to crank every knob just to get something beautiful.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-13

“Old parameter habits do not transfer perfectly. Every major model teaches you a new way to drive.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-13

## Draft mode 改变了 prompting 方法，Midjourney 开始从出图工具走向实时创作对话

Draft mode 在这期里被反复讨论。主持人把它理解成一种新的 prompting workflow：用户可以更快试方向、用语音引导、快速排除错误路线。Midjourney 如果要进入更专业的创作流程，速度和反馈周期会越来越关键。高质量模型负责最终资产，draft mode 负责探索空间；二者结合之后，产品从“提交 prompt 等结果”转向“边想边试”的创作对话。这是 Midjourney 往工作流工具升级的重要信号。

“Draft mode is a different way of prompting. It is faster, looser, and it lets you guide the model while you are still figuring out what you want.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-13

“It already feels better than at launch. Final quality is not the point here; speed of exploration is.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-13

“Voice and draft change the rhythm. Instead of writing a perfect prompt, you can talk your way into the direction.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-13

## Midjourney 改成 drip release，会让社区持续测试

这期材料提到 Midjourney 将以每周或隔周的方式慢慢释放功能，把 remix、tile 等能力陆续补回。这个节奏变化很重要：大版本发布会制造声量，但也会带来高预期和集中失望；drip release 能让社区持续测试、持续生成教程、持续讨论。对小团队而言，这也是一种现实的产品管理方式：先放出可用部分，利用社区反馈确定下一步，避免把所有能力都压到一次完美发布里。

“Midjourney said new things would drip out weekly or every other week. Remix, tile and other pieces will come back gradually.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-13

“That cadence matters because the community keeps testing. People do not disappear after one big release; they come back when something new drops.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-13

“The model launch is not the end of the work. The real work is rebuilding the feature set around the new architecture.” —— Midjourney Fast Hours, 2025-04-13
